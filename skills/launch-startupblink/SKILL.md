# Launch on StartupBlink

## Goal

Create `.ulpi/launch/startupblink/` with `ROUTE-AND-ELIGIBILITY.md`, `BENCHMARK.md`, `COMPANY-PACKAGE.md`,
`LOCATION-AND-ECOSYSTEM.md`, `RANKING-AND-PROMOTION-BOUNDARY.md`, `CORRECTION-RUNBOOK.md`, `CHECKLIST.md`,
`analytics.md`, and shared positioning.

## Workflow

### 1. Prove current listing fit

Ask for official name/domain, public startup status, exact operating location, sector, founding date, public
description, ecosystem affiliation, approved founder/team facts, existing profile, source URLs, owner, and desired
route. Return `READY TO SUBMIT/CLAIM`, `READY TO CORRECT`, or `NOT ELIGIBLE — NO CURRENT ROUTE`.

### 2. Benchmark five live peers

Choose five by sector, geography, stage, and local ecosystem. Capture observed profile, location/sector framing,
ecosystem/ranking context, proof, and platform-generated labels. Keep public facts distinct from inference.

### 3. Prepare source-backed company data

After authorized form observation, map every live field to an approved source: company identity, website,
description, sector, city/country, founded date, public team, approved funding/status, and logo/media. Maintain a
location-evidence and ranking/promotion boundary ledger.

### 4. Submit and maintain responsibly

Present exact fields, sources, location choice, promotion option, recipients, and submitter for approval. Track
approval, accuracy, country/city/sector discovery, referrals, corrections, and material changes—never attribute rank
movement to the listing without platform evidence. End `LIVE — STARTUPBLINK PROFILE GOVERNED`.
