# Launch on VentureRadar

## Goal

Create `.ulpi/launch/ventureradar/` with `ROUTE.md`, `BENCHMARK.md`, `COMPANY-PACKAGE.md`,
`INTELLIGENCE-DATA-BOUNDARY.md`, `CORRECTION-RUNBOOK.md`, `CHECKLIST.md`, `analytics.md`, and shared positioning.

## Workflow

### 1. Resolve route and authority

Ask for official name/domain, public legal/entity status, founding year, country, approved company description,
keywords/categories, founder/team permission, approved funding disclosure, profile owner, sources, existing URL, and
change/correction owner. Browse current route and return `READY TO SUBMIT`, `READY TO CLAIM/CORRECT`, or `BLOCKED`.

### 2. Benchmark five startup profiles

Choose five peers by sector, stage, geography, buyer, and maturity. Record public profile structure, positioning,
identity fields, website, category signals, evidence, and what is platform-generated. Do not infer funding or traction.

### 3. Prepare a field-ready profile

After authorized form inspection, map each live field to a sourced approved value: company identity, legal/status,
website, founded year, location, description, category/keywords, founders/team only if public, approved facts, logo,
and references. Create a public-vs-confidential data boundary and correction evidence pack.

### 4. Submit, correct, and maintain

Show exact fields, source ledger, disclosure choices, and submitter for approval. Track approval, profile accuracy,
search/referral quality, corrections, update latency, and investor-facing discrepancies. End `LIVE — VENTURERADAR
PROFILE GOVERNED`.
