# Launch on Peerlist Launchpad

## Goal

Create `.ulpi/launch/peerlist-launchpad/` with `ROUTE-AND-READINESS.md`, `BENCHMARK.md`, `PROJECT-PACKET.md`,
`MAKER-PROFILE-PACKET.md`, `WEEKLY-SCHEDULE.md`, `FORM-CAPTURE.md`, `COMMUNITY-RUNBOOK.md`, `analytics.md`, and
positioning.

## Workflow

### 1. Confirm maker and weekly-launch fit

Return `WEEKLY LAUNCH READY`, `PROFILE FIRST`, `FEATURED AD OPTION TO REVIEW`, or `NOT READY`. Confirm the product is
testable, project ownership/maker identity is accurate, a support owner is available for the launch window, and a
professional profile is appropriate.

### 2. Benchmark exactly five peers

Use `$browse` to compare five current/recent, genuinely comparable projects. Extract observable title/tagline,
categories, maker profile framing, product proof, rating/vote/comment context, and CTA. Build differentiated factual
copy and a developer/designer-focused feedback request.

### 3. Build project and maker packets

Prepare canonical name/domain, customer/problem/solution, concise description, categories, access/pricing, logo/
screenshots/demo, public maker/team identity, support URL, changelog, source register, and UTMs. Keep claims, team
details, and media rights verified. Create feedback prompts that invite independent testing without requesting votes.

### 4. Capture live account and featured-ad details

After explicit authorization, record all current profile/launch fields, Monday timing, validation, publication, and
community rules. If considering `Book your slot`/advertising, capture exact price/currency/tax, duration, placement,
disclosure, payment/refund, and scope separately from organic weekly ranking.

### 5. Publish with approval and operate

Present field-to-source map, weekly schedule, launch preview, community reply plan, and any ad terms for approval.
After authorized publication, verify project page, labels, links/UTMs, timing, and actual community interaction; correct
facts and measure qualified referrals, activation, feedback quality, conversion, and spend. End `LIVE — PEERLIST
LAUNCHPAD RUNBOOK ACTIVE`.
