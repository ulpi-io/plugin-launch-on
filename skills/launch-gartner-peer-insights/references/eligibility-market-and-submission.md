# Gartner Peer Insights Eligibility, Market, and Submission

Official live listing guidance observed July 23, 2026 requires the owning vendor, an independently sellable
suite/highest service level, mid-to-large enterprise customers, all mandatory market capabilities, and at least
50% of other market capabilities. Resellers/integrators/partners cannot host listings.

Request fields explicitly listed: contact name, title/role, email; company name; product name; public product
website; requested active market; and explanation of market fit. Capture additional live portal fields.

Use Gartner naming rules: beta as `Product (beta)`; suite-level rather than modules; public evidence for
renames/M&A; legacy as `Product (Legacy)` after public EOL/EOS evidence. Search duplicates first.

For market disputes, use public website/press release/downloadable datasheet evidence, not internal application
documents. Gartner controls classification, reclassification, migration, and removal.
