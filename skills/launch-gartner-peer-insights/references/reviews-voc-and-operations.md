# Gartner Reviews, Voice of Customer, and Operations

Reviewers must author feedback from direct personal experience, comply with employer policy/confidentiality,
and not be employed by the vendor, competitor, or an organization with an exclusive partner/reseller/integrator/
consultant relationship. Never ghostwrite, gate, alter, or pressure reviews.

Use only Gartner's Vendor Portal campaign and Gartner-administered incentives. The current vendor overview
advertises complimentary gift cards for sourcing reviews; verify live value, country, recipient, disclosure,
campaign, and privacy rules. Never independently incentivize.

Verify current Customers' Choice and Voice of the Customer methodology. Record eligibility window, minimum
reviews, recency, market coverage/distribution, freeze period, and next cycle. New reviews during a freeze may
count only in the next cycle. Gartner-generated rating, rank, insights, reports, and distinctions are not
promisable.

Audit market fit, product status, evidence, profile, reviews, access, and privacy quarterly. Maintain public
EOL/EOS and M&A evidence and respond to Gartner requests promptly.
