# Gartner Peer Insights Profile, Benchmark, and Copy

Benchmark exactly five live relevant products in the target market. Record default sort, filters, date/timezone,
market definition/transition, displayed order, rating/count, distinctions, overview, alternatives, review
segments, integration/deployment signals, pricing/screenshots, vendor details, and public-site consistency.

Profile content must be English, third person, factual, publicly verifiable, grammatically standard, and limited
to the approved company/product. Do not include first person, superlatives, competitor names, awards, CTAs,
links, phone/email/PII, line breaks, symbols, special characters, HTML, offensive/defamatory content, or
marketing screens. Use actual product screenshots.

Vendor Profile contains auto-populated company information plus company description and portal facts such as
revenue/employees when offered. Product Profile is completed separately for each product in each market and may
include product description, pricing, screenshots, and current portal fields.
