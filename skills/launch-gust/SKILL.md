1. GUST IS FUNDRAISING AND VENTURE OPERATIONS, NOT A GENERAL DIRECTORY. The July 23, 2026 site describes a Gust
   Company Profile used for accelerators, investor/VC matching, applications, diligence, cap tables, and fundraising.
   Require a real authorized company and relevant capital/program goal; do not create a profile for backlink value.
2. PROTECT SENSITIVE INFORMATION. Treat cap table, ownership, terms, investor conversations, revenue, runway,
   customer data, financial model, legal documents, and diligence assets as confidential. Do not upload, submit,
   share, or expose anything without explicit owner and legal/finance approval.
3. USE $browse AND EXACTLY FIVE PEERS/PROGRAMS. Inspect current Gust routes and five comparable public startup,
   accelerator, or funding-program surfaces. Capture only public positioning, stage, vertical, application cues, and
   proof. Use $browse-stealth/Camoufox if challenged; do not invent account-gated fields.
4. NO FUNDRAISING MISREPRESENTATION. Never fabricate traction, revenue, customers, pipeline, incorporation, team,
   ownership, valuation, terms, IP, compliance, fundraise status, investor interest, or endorsements. Separate public
   profile facts from program-specific and confidential investor materials.
5. HUMAN APPROVAL REQUIRED. Never create/login, submit applications, connect with investors, set terms, sign SAFEs or
   notes, update cap tables, or share documents without explicit approval. Capture all live form fields after approval.

# Launch on Gust

## Goal

Create `.ulpi/launch/gust/` with `ROUTE-AND-ELIGIBILITY.md`, `BENCHMARK.md`, `COMPANY-PROFILE.md`,
`FUNDRAISING-READINESS.md`, `PROGRAM-APPLICATIONS.md`, `DILIGENCE-AND-DATA-ROOM.md`, `GOVERNANCE.md`,
`CHECKLIST.md`, `analytics.md`, and shared positioning.

## Workflow

### 1. Establish fundraising authority and fit

Ask for company/entity status, founders/authority, approved public profile facts, target programs/investor types,
stage, raise objective, legal/finance owner, cap-table owner, diligence-data policy, existing Gust URL, and deadlines.
Browse live routes and return `READY TO BUILD PROFILE`, `READY TO APPLY`, or `NOT READY — AUTHORITY/DILIGENCE NEEDED`.

### 2. Benchmark five peers or programs

Choose five close public peers/programs by stage, sector, geography, and funding path. Record public positioning,
eligibility, milestones, application cues, and proof—without inferring fundraising or private details.

### 3. Build separated public and confidential packages

After authorized live-form capture, prepare public company identity, concise description, market/problem/outcome,
team, stage, approved traction, and official links. Separately prepare application/diligence answer mapping, evidence
index, confidentiality classification, owner approvals, and deadlines. Never combine restricted documents with public
content by default.

### 4. Submit and operate under controls

Before any action, present exact fields, recipients, confidential documents, permissions, terms, and submitter for
approval. Track application status, requests, meetings, conversion, due diligence, compliance, time-to-close, and
fundraising outcome without disclosing restricted data. End `LIVE — GUST FUNDRAISING OPERATIONS GOVERNED`.
