# Launch on TechPluto

## Goal

Create `.ulpi/launch/techpluto/` with `ROUTE-AND-FIT.md`, `BENCHMARK.md`, `EDITORIAL-PITCH.md`, `SOURCE-PACKET.md`,
`FORM-CAPTURE.md`, `COMMERCIAL-DISCLOSURE.md`, `FOLLOW-UP-RUNBOOK.md`, `CHECKLIST.md`, `analytics.md`, and shared
positioning.

## Workflow

### 1. Establish section and news fit

Collect the story hook, official product/company sources, founder authority, customer permissions, launch date or
embargo, relevant TechPluto section, factual milestones, approved media, and commercial budget. Browse live routes
and return `READY TO PITCH`, `READY TO REVIEW PAID OPTION`, or `NOT NEWSWORTHY`.

### 2. Benchmark exactly five peer surfaces

Choose five relevant current articles, profiles, interviews, or reviews. Record section, title, author/date,
structure, evidence, claims, reader value, disclosure, CTA, and discussion context. Derive an original pitch shape;
never copy copy or treat prior coverage as an entitlement.

### 3. Build the editor-ready packet

Prepare a concise subject line, news hook, one-sentence relevance, factual summary, primary source links, approved
quote, founder/company bio, technical/product facts, visual assets, suggested section, correction contact, and a
clear disclosure of any commercial relationship. Capture all current submit-route fields after approval.

### 4. Submit and measure with approval

Show exact outreach, form values, source packet, disclosure, costs, and owner for approval. After publication,
verify title, category, link, dates, claims, labels, and corrections. Track referral quality, qualified conversion,
engagement, source accuracy, response/publication status, and paid spend separately. End `LIVE — TECHPLUTO RUNBOOK ACTIVE`.
