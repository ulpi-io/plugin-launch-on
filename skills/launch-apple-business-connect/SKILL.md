# Launch on Apple Business Connect

## Goal

Create `.ulpi/launch/apple-business-connect/` with `ROUTE-AND-ELIGIBILITY.md`, `BENCHMARK.md`,
`BUSINESS-OR-BRAND-PACKAGE.md`, `MAPS-AND-ACTIONS.md`, `VERIFICATION.md`, `GOVERNANCE.md`, `CHECKLIST.md`,
`analytics.md`, and shared positioning.

## Workflow

### 1. Resolve business or brand eligibility

Ask for legal/consumer name, local operations or qualifying brand, locations/service areas, category, hours, phone,
website, brand rights, logo/media, custom-action target, owner/admins, verification contact, regional availability,
existing Apple listing, and approved proof. Browse the current route and return `READY TO ENROLL`, `READY TO CLAIM`,
or `NOT ELIGIBLE — NO CURRENT APPLE ROUTE`.

### 2. Benchmark five live peers

Choose five peers by buyer, category, region, local/brand model, and action type. Record observed naming, visual
assets, Maps data, actions, category, proof, and friction. Label observed fact vs inference and do not copy assets or
copywriting.

### 3. Prepare exact data, assets, and actions

After authorized live form observation, prepare every field: identity, business/brand relationship, locations/service
areas, category, hours/special hours, phone, website, descriptions, logo/cover/photos, product/service details,
custom actions, destination URLs, and verification evidence. Test action destinations for accessibility, privacy, and
accurate availability.

### 4. Enroll and operate with approval

Show exact fields, location visibility, action destination, assets, verification contact, admins, and owner for
approval. Track approval, Maps discovery, action clicks, booking/order/contact conversions, regional performance,
asset freshness, and accuracy incidents. End `LIVE — APPLE BUSINESS PRESENCE GOVERNED`.
