<EXTREMELY-IMPORTANT>
1. REQUIRE LOCAL ELIGIBILITY. The July 23, 2026 Google Business Profile page states eligibility for a physical
   storefront or a service-area business that interacts in person with customers. A purely online SaaS without such
   operations is not eligible. Return `NOT ELIGIBLE — NO CUSTOMER-FACING LOCAL OPERATION`; never use a virtual office
   or fabricated address.
2. USE $browse AND EXACTLY FIVE LOCAL PEERS. Inspect the live setup route and five comparable Maps/Search profiles.
   Capture category, address/service area, hours, phone, attributes, photos, services/products, reviews, posts, Q&A,
   booking/order actions, and CTA as publicly observed. Use $browse-stealth/Camoufox if challenged.
3. NO FALSE BUSINESS DATA. Never invent name, location, service area, phone, hours, category, accessibility/identity
   attributes, products/services, availability, review, photos, customers, or local presence. Use the real-world
   operating location and exact legal/consumer-facing details.
4. VERIFICATION, REVIEWS, AND USER DATA REQUIRE APPROVAL. Never create/login, claim/verify/edit a profile, respond to
   reviews/Q&A, publish offers/posts, or contact customers without explicit approval. Capture every current live field
   and verification requirement after authorized login. Do not incentivize positive reviews or gate service on reviews.
5. GOVERN THE PROFILE. Define owner, managers, address/privacy handling, holiday-hours source, incident/escalation
   owner, review-response SLA, and change log. Make emergency closures and availability updates truthful and prompt.
</EXTREMELY-IMPORTANT>

# Launch on Google Business Profile

## Goal

Create `.ulpi/launch/google-business-profile/` with `ELIGIBILITY.md`, `BENCHMARK.md`, `PROFILE-PACKAGE.md`,
`VERIFICATION.md`, `LOCAL-CONTENT.md`, `REVIEW-AND-QA-OPERATIONS.md`, `GOVERNANCE.md`, `CHECKLIST.md`,
`analytics.md`, and shared positioning.

## Workflow

### 1. Prove local fit

Ask for legal/consumer-facing name, physical location or service area, in-person customer interaction, category,
hours, phone, website, services/products, actual attributes, photo permission, owner/manager, existing profile, and
verification contact. Browse current setup. Return `READY TO CLAIM/VERIFY`, `READY TO CREATE`, or `NOT ELIGIBLE`.

### 2. Benchmark five live local peers

Pick five real local/service-area peers by customer, category, geography, and operating model. Record observed data,
visuals, category use, review themes, posts/offers, services, Q&A, and CTA. Identify completeness gaps without copying
copywriting or claiming their facts.

### 3. Prepare exact profile and verification data

After authorized form inspection, prepare every live field: name, category, address/service area, pin, hours/special
hours, phone, website, services/products, attributes, description, logo/cover/photos, booking/order links, opening
date only if true, and managers. Create verification evidence, holiday-hours calendar, review/Q&A response templates,
and a source ledger.

### 4. Verify, publish, and maintain

Show exact fields, address visibility, verification recipient, assets, posts/offers, and response owner for approval.
Track verification, Search/Maps views, calls, direction requests, website clicks, bookings/orders, review trend,
response time, Q&A, and local conversion. End `LIVE — BUSINESS PROFILE GOVERNED`.
