# Launch on The Org

## Goal

Create `.ulpi/launch/the-org/` with `ROUTE-AND-ELIGIBILITY.md`, `BENCHMARK.md`, `COMPANY-PACKAGE.md`,
`PUBLIC-ORG-CHART.md`, `PRIVACY-AND-GOVERNANCE.md`, `CHANGE-RUNBOOK.md`, `CHECKLIST.md`, `analytics.md`, and shared
positioning.

## Workflow

### 1. Establish public-org authority

Ask for official name/domain, company description, industry, public locations, approved size band, approved public
leaders/teams, relationship visibility, profile owner, HR/comms approver, employee-removal path, existing URL, and
source-of-truth system. Return `READY TO CREATE/CLAIM`, `READY TO UPDATE`, or `BLOCKED — AUTHORITY/CONSENT NEEDED`.

### 2. Benchmark five public companies

Choose peers by industry, size, geography, and org complexity. Record observed company data, leadership/team display,
content depth, and public proof. Mark all observations and do not infer missing people or reporting lines.

### 3. Prepare field-ready public content

After authorized live form observation, map fields for company identity, description, industry, location, size band,
logo, public leaders, teams, roles, approved relationships, careers/website, and update sources. Include a per-person
approval/status register and correction/removal workflow.

### 4. Publish and govern

Present exact fields, every person/role/relation, approval state, admins, and update cadence for approval. Track
profile status, view/referral quality, accuracy corrections, update latency, employee requests, and access changes.
End `LIVE — PUBLIC ORG PROFILE GOVERNED`.
