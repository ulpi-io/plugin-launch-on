# Launch on F6S

## Goal

Create `.ulpi/launch/f6s/` with `ROUTE-AND-ELIGIBILITY.md`, `BENCHMARK.md`, `COMPANY-PACKAGE.md`,
`OPPORTUNITY-PACKAGE.md`, `EVIDENCE-ROOM.md`, `APPLICATION-RUNBOOK.md`, `CHECKLIST.md`, `analytics.md`, and shared
positioning.

## Workflow

### 1. Select the appropriate live route

Ask for product/company URL, legal entity and location, founder authority, funding/stage, sector, hiring status,
program/deal/event target, deadlines, required documents, budget, existing F6S URL, and approved proof. Browse current
public navigation and return one of `COMPANY PROFILE`, `PROGRAM APPLICATION`, `STARTUP DEAL`, `EVENT`, `JOB`,
`CLAIM/UPDATE`, or `NOT ELIGIBLE` with evidence.

### 2. Benchmark five comparable live entries

Use F6S public company collections and the specific opportunity surface. Record five peer URLs, positioning, target
market, stage/location signals, proof, visual/media pattern, application requirements where public, and CTA. Label
each observation `LIVE-FORM`, `PUBLIC PROFILE`, `PROGRAM-SPECIFIC`, `INFERRED`, or `UNKNOWN`.

### 3. Assemble truthful field-ready materials

Prepare official company name/domain, concise promise, problem/customer/outcome, founder/team identities, industry,
location, stage, business model, approved traction, logo/screenshots, company/about text, links, and contact owner.
For a specific program, create answers mapped one-to-one to the current live questions plus evidence/attachment index,
deadline, eligibility notes, risks, and owner sign-off. Capture actual account-gated fields only after authorization.

### 4. Submit and operate only with approval

Present a final field table, attachments, public/private classification, program terms, deadline, and submitter before
any action. Track application status, requests, interview/meeting dates, program-specific outcome, attributed traffic,
qualified leads, deal savings, and hiring results separately. Keep an evidence log; do not reapply, spam organizers, or
claim affiliation. End `LIVE — F6S ROUTE OPERATING` or `NOT ELIGIBLE — [REASON]`.
