# Launch on Top Startups

## Goal

Create `.ulpi/launch/top-startups/` with `ROUTE-AND-ELIGIBILITY.md`, `BENCHMARK.md`, `LISTING-FACT-SHEET.md`,
`COMMERCIAL-DECISION.md`, `JOB-ADD-ON-PACKET.md`, `FORM-CAPTURE.md`, `SOURCE-REGISTER.md`, `analytics.md`, and
positioning.

## Workflow

### 1. Choose the commercial route

Return `STARTUP LISTING OPTION READY`, `LISTING-PLUS-JOBS OPTION READY`, `WAIT FOR EVIDENCE`, or `NOT A FIT`. Confirm
the company is an eligible high-growth startup, its site works, relevant facts are source-backed, and the $99/$249
options are still live. Assess whether jobs are real, authorized openings rather than a reason to buy the bundle.

### 2. Benchmark exactly five cards

Use `$browse` to inspect five comparable current cards and the closest live filters. Compare only observable fields:
what-they-do sentence, tags, HQ, team-size band, founded year, stage/funding, investor attribution, founder signals,
featured/review/jobs links, and CTA. Produce a differentiated factual positioning hierarchy.

### 3. Create the listing and jobs packets

Prepare canonical name/domain, one concise description, industry tags, HQ/remote status, headcount range, founding
year, product/category, stage, disclosed funding and investor sources, founder details, logo/media if requested,
company/LinkedIn/careers URLs, and correction owner. For jobs, prepare approved title, location/work type, employment
type, salary/equity only when authorized, role description, application destination, and expiry owner.

### 4. Capture live purchase and follow-up fields

Record the exact work-email, website, package, payment, terms, refund, tax, follow-up, and publication fields. Build a
field-to-source map and a clear paid-placement disclosure. Do not equate any card placement or `Featured` treatment
with investor endorsement.

### 5. Approve, submit, and operate

Present the selected package, current price/currency/taxes, exact form preview, source register, payment owner, and
job scope for approval. After authorized submission/purchase, save receipts and confirmation, verify published facts
and UTM links, correct drift with evidence, maintain only live jobs, and measure qualified referrals, application
starts, profile accuracy, and spend. End `LIVE — TOP STARTUPS RUNBOOK ACTIVE`.
