# Launch on Comparably

## Goal

Create `.ulpi/launch/comparably/` with `ROUTE-AND-ELIGIBILITY.md`, `BENCHMARK.md`, `EMPLOYER-PACKAGE.md`,
`CULTURE-AND-CANDIDATE-EXPERIENCE.md`, `REVIEW-QA-SALARY-GOVERNANCE.md`, `AWARDS-AND-PAID.md`, `CHECKLIST.md`,
`analytics.md`, and shared positioning.

## Workflow

### 1. Resolve route and authority

Ask for legal/display name, company facts, careers/jobs, locations, actual culture/benefits, leader authority,
employer-brand assets, HR/legal/privacy owners, existing profile, award claims, and budget. Browse live surface and
return `READY TO CLAIM/UPDATE`, `READY FOR EMPLOYER PRESENCE`, or `BLOCKED — LIVE ROUTE OR AUTHORITY NEEDED`.

### 2. Benchmark five employers

Choose five comparable companies by hiring market, size, region, and work model. Record observed culture/story,
reviews/ratings, salaries/Q&A, recruiting CTA, media, awards, and response patterns. Keep fact/inference separate.

### 3. Prepare approved employer content

After authorized form capture, map every field to approved evidence: profile/about, mission, product/team, locations,
work model, culture/values, leadership, benefits, careers/jobs, assets, and factual candidate FAQs. Build a safe
response/escalation rubric for reviews, salary topics, Q&A, discrimination, safety, legal, and personnel matters.

### 4. Govern and measure

Present all fields, assets, public replies, paid/award options, owners, and privacy implications for approval. Track
claim status, qualified applies, source quality, candidate experience, review/Q&A themes, brand-health changes, and
paid spend. End `LIVE — COMPARABLY EMPLOYER PRESENCE GOVERNED`.
