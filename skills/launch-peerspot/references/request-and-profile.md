# PeerSpot Request and Profile

## Request route

Use the live vendor contact page. On July 23, 2026 the rendered form showed First Name, Last Name, Email,
Company Name, AWS Marketplace and Google Cloud Marketplace status, Area of Interest, and Message. `Listing my
product/company` was one Area of Interest option. Follow-on listing/onboarding fields are platform-owned: capture
them live rather than guessing.

## Product identity

PeerSpot's public surface is enterprise technology buying intelligence: product reviews, categories, comparisons,
questions, buyer guides, and vendor programs. List a distinct product and its owning/authorized vendor, not a
module, service, reseller, or duplicate. Search product/company variants and comparisons before a new request.

## Public profile evidence

Current product pages commonly expose vendor/product name, category, reviews, reviewer context, review use case,
pros, cons, ROI, alternatives, and comparisons. Possible editable/vendor-managed fields must be verified in the
current onboarding or vendor relationship. Attribute platform-generated ratings, review extracts, rankings, and
buyer data to PeerSpot—not to the vendor.

## Privacy

Do not keep customer lists, credentials, contract details, or reviewers' personal data in package artifacts.
Record only owners, purpose, minimal data class, retention, and evidence.
