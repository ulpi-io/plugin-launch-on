# PeerSpot Reviews, Community, and Operations

## Authentic reviews

Invite the complete eligible cohort after a defined genuine experience using neutral wording. Exclude conflicts,
employees, fabricated identities, and people whose employers forbid participation. Do not gate by sentiment,
NPS, support result, plan, spend, or geography. Respect the current review form and reviewer verification.

PeerSpot may offer vendor-managed review capture or incentives. Verify that current official program before use;
never independently promise money, gifts, discounts, credits, or rewards for reviews, ratings, or edits.

## Community

Questions, answers, comparisons, and discussion are buyer community surfaces. A vendor can participate only
transparently as an affiliated representative, with accurate helpful information and no manipulation, attacks,
fake accounts, vote coordination, or reviewer pressure. Escalate safety, legal, or PII issues.

## Operations

Weekly: critical reviews/questions, broken CTAs, security/pricing changes. Monthly: content freshness, referrals,
review coverage and recency, comparison/category accuracy. Quarterly: categories, alternatives, access, privacy,
marketplace status, vendor-program ROI, and incident rehearsal.

Measure referral/CTA conversion, review workflow, community SLA, category/comparison exposure, marketplace
referrals, and first-party outcomes. Keep organic positioning distinct from any paid program.
