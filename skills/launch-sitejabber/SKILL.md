# Launch on SmartCustomer / Sitejabber

## Goal

Create `.ulpi/launch/sitejabber/` with `ROUTE-AND-OWNERSHIP.md`, `BENCHMARK.md`, `PROFILE-PACKET.md`,
`REVIEW-PROGRAM.md`, `RESPONSE-GOVERNANCE.md`, `ACCOUNT-FORM-CAPTURE.md`, `INTEGRATION-AND-SPEND.md`, `analytics.md`,
and positioning.

## Workflow

### 1. Resolve the profile and ownership route

Return `CLAIM READY`, `PROFILE ALREADY MANAGED`, `IDENTITY/DOMAIN PROOF NEEDED`, or `NOT READY`. Search canonical
brand/domain and variants; verify the business actually controls the destination, public contact details, and customer
support escalation. Choose claim/correction rather than duplicate creation.

### 2. Benchmark exactly five live profiles

Use `$browse` to compare exactly five current category peers. Extract their factual profile composition, rating context,
review transparency, themes, response practices, and conversion elements. Build verified, differentiated profile copy;
never use peer reviews or outcomes as promised targets.

### 3. Prepare a truthful profile and review program

Create a source-governed packet for brand/legal name, canonical website, category, concise description, logo, customer
support URL, privacy/returns/terms where relevant, product/service scope, and contact owner. Define an equal-opportunity
review invite triggered by a real completed interaction, neutral copy, no sentiment filter, no reward tied to a rating,
and a respectful opt-out. Keep customer data minimised and consented.

### 4. Capture the account controls and approve the preview

After authorization, claim the free profile and record the current registration, verification, business fields,
integrations, widgets, invitation/automation controls, reviewer-data consent, review dispute/reporting process, plans,
price/currency/tax, renewal/cancellation, and public-response controls. Map every entry to a source and present the
profile, invite, and commercial terms for approval.

### 5. Operate reputation with integrity

After authorized activation, verify the live profile/domain/category and invite path. Monitor genuine feedback; respond
with facts, empathy, and a private support route without publishing personal data. Use reporting/dispute processes only
for actual policy violations, document all actions, correct verified mistakes, and track review coverage, response time,
support resolution, referral quality, and conversion without claiming causation. End `LIVE — SMARTCUSTOMER REPUTATION
RUNBOOK ACTIVE`.
