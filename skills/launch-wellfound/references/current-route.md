offerings include free job posting and applicant tracking, AI candidate sourcing, and managed recruiting. It states
that candidates can create profiles and apply for free. Current public page copy says paid/promoted/managed services
are distinct from free job posting.

The legacy assumption that Wellfound is a generic startup-directory backlink route must not be made. Verify the
authenticated employer/company UI before promising a public company profile. A non-hiring company without an available
public profile route is not eligible for a Wellfound listing workflow.
