# Launch on Uneed

## Goal

Create `.ulpi/launch/uneed/` with `ROUTE-AND-READINESS.md`, `BENCHMARK.md`, `LAUNCH-PACKET.md`,
`SCRAPED-DATA-VERIFICATION.md`, `COMMERCIAL-DECISION.md`, `FORM-CAPTURE.md`, `COMMUNITY-RUNBOOK.md`, `analytics.md`,
and positioning.

## Workflow

### 1. Select a launch route

Return `FREE QUEUE READY`, `SKIP-THE-LINE OPTION TO REVIEW`, `RELAUNCH READY`, `PRO OPTION TO REVIEW`, `AD/REVIEW
OPTION TO REVIEW`, or `NOT LAUNCH-READY`. Confirm the product is live, canonical URL is crawlable, owner is authorized,
and the desired date/visibility outcome is realistic.

### 2. Benchmark exactly five peers

Use `$browse` to study five comparable current listings. Record observable title/tagline, category/tags, display type,
rank/votes/reviews/badges, product access, and CTA. Build a truthful category and launch narrative, never a plan to
manipulate ladders or use peer metrics as claims.

### 3. Verify scraped data and build assets

Prepare canonical product name/domain, concise positioning, customer/problem/solution, tags/categories, access/pricing,
logo/screenshots/video, makers, public support/correction contact, source register, and UTMs. When Uneed scrapes the
product page, compare every generated field against these sources and correct errors before save/publish.

### 4. Re-capture commercial terms and review boundaries

For any paid route, document live total cost, currency/tax, queue/launch date, feature/ad duration, profile/link
treatment, product review scope/independence, cancellation/refund, and disclosure label. A paid public review must be
handled as independent editorial testing; do not request a positive result or edit conclusions beyond factual fixes.

### 5. Publish with approval and operate

Present the form preview, scraper corrections, selected route, current terms, asset rights, and community plan for
approval. After authorized launch, verify listing, labels, links/UTMs, date, and analytics; engage candidly, correct
facts, and measure qualified referrals, feedback, activation, conversion, and spend. End `LIVE — UNEED RUNBOOK ACTIVE`.
