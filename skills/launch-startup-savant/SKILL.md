# Launch on Startup Savant

## Goal

Create `.ulpi/launch/startup-savant/` with `ROUTE-AND-FIT.md`, `BENCHMARK.md`, `EDITORIAL-PITCH.md`,
`SOURCE-PACKET.md`, `PAID-DISCLOSURE.md`, `FOLLOW-UP-RUNBOOK.md`, `CHECKLIST.md`, `analytics.md`, and shared
positioning.

## Workflow

### 1. Determine editorial fit

Ask for story/event, public sources, founder/company authority, customer permission, date/embargo, audience, proposed
angle, relevant Startup Savant section, existing coverage, and paid budget. Browse live routes and return `READY TO
PITCH`, `READY TO EVALUATE ADVERTISING`, or `NOT NEWSWORTHY FOR EDITORIAL`.

### 2. Benchmark five editorial examples

Select five relevant profiles, watchlist entries, features, or podcasts. Record headline/angle, evidence, format,
voice, disclosure, and reader takeaway. Do not copy their text or treat their inclusion as a template entitlement.

### 3. Assemble an editor-ready package

Create concise pitch options, one-sentence relevance, factual summary, primary source links, approved quotes, founder
bio, factual milestones, media, category/section fit, disclosure, and correction contact. Keep paid options separately
labelled with terms and no editorial-guarantee claim.

### 4. Submit and measure with approval

Present exact outreach, assets, sources, disclosures, and owner for approval. Track response, publication status,
accuracy corrections, referred sessions, qualified conversion, source quality, and paid spend separately. End `LIVE —
STARTUP SAVANT EDITORIAL RUNBOOK ACTIVE`.
