# Launch on Vestbee

## Goal

Create `.ulpi/launch/vestbee/` with `ROUTE-AND-ELIGIBILITY.md`, `BENCHMARK.md`, `PROFILE-SOURCE-REGISTER.md`,
`ONE-PAGER.md`, `INVESTOR-READINESS.md`, `PROGRAM-APPLICATION-PACKET.md`, `FORM-CAPTURE.md`, `analytics.md`, and
positioning.

## Workflow

### 1. Resolve the route and eligibility

Choose `STARTUP PROFILE READY`, `FUNDRAISING ONE-PAGER READY`, `PROGRAM APPLICATION READY`, `COMMUNITY/EVENT READY`,
or `NOT YET A FIT`. Confirm a real company, authorized representative, live product or credible launch plan, relevant
market/geography, and whether the selected route requires fundraising readiness.

### 2. Research exactly five peer surfaces

Use `$browse` to benchmark five live, comparable Vestbee surfaces. Separate observed facts from inference. Distill the
peer patterns into a category-aware headline, one-sentence positioning, proof hierarchy, concise business model,
traction language, investor fit, and CTA—without copying a peer's wording or unverified claims.

### 3. Capture the live form and create truthful assets

After account authorization, record the actual field names, help text, required markers, character limits, file
requirements, privacy settings, review/verification gates, and confirmation state. Build a complete fact sheet:

- company name, canonical domain, logo, product screenshots, founder/authorized representative, headquarters and
  operating geographies;
- category, customer, problem, solution, market, business model, competition/alternatives, and product status;
- fundraising stage, raise target, use of funds, traction, revenue/customer/funding claims, and sources only where
  approved; and
- a public-safe startup one-pager plus a separately governed investor version, each with a correction owner.

### 4. Prepare the fundraising and program packet

Tailor the one-pager to the selected investor/program thesis: concise problem, solution, why-now, market, validation,
team, ask, and next step. Map eligibility, deadline, geography, stage, sector, equity/fee terms, data-sharing scope,
and selection process for each program. Treat any media coverage, perks, credits, events, or introductions as
opportunities rather than outcomes.

### 5. Submit only with approval and operate

Before any state change, present the exact field-to-source mapping, public/private boundary, selected route, program
terms, and submission preview for approval. After approval, verify the live profile or application receipt, use secure
links only for authorized recipients, respond to platform requests with corrected evidence, and measure profile views,
qualified investor conversations, applications, introductions, event participation, and attributable referrals. End
`LIVE — VESTBEE RUNBOOK ACTIVE`.
