# SaaSHub eligibility, submission, and verification

## Current submission gate

The observed SaaSHub Submit a Product page (July 23, 2026) begins with **Website URL**, requires approval for every
submission, and says to add a few relevant categories and listed competitors. It warns that missing listed
competitors slow a submission and put it at the bottom of the queue. It rejects:

- software development agencies
- waitlist-only landing pages
- unreleased products
- products on free subdomains
- products not in English

It says it accepts SaaS, IaaS, PaaS, most software/apps, mobile apps with decent websites, and niche-leading sites.
Treat this as a live gate: capture the full current form and requirements in-browser before submission.

## Verification handoff

SaaSHub’s current Product Verification page describes three access methods: company email, an HTML snippet on the
product website, or a support-requested verification link. Once an authorized vendor has access, review the current
Verification tab and verify the details. The documentation describes a verification badge, visibility on competitor
and category pages, and a quarterly update reminder. None guarantees traffic, rank, or approval.
