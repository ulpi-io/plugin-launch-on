# SaaSworthy profile research

Current SaaSworthy public pages show software identity/overview, category, features, pricing plans and options,
screenshots, alternatives, reviews, FAQ, CTA/contact, and sometimes customers and integrations. The marketplace
also displays pricing insights and a disclaimer that a listed price may differ from the vendor’s current price.
Therefore capture every current form field live, retain a first-party price source and date, and request a correction
when a public value is stale.

Use `$browse` for the exact-five category benchmark. For each peer record page URL, category, buyer job, overview,
features, plan names/prices, trials, screenshots, integrations, alternatives, reviews/FAQ themes, CTA, and a lesson
for a more accurate—not copied—profile.
