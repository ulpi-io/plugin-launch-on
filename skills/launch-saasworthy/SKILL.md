# Launch on SaaSworthy

## Required output

Write `.ulpi/launch/saasworthy/ELIGIBILITY-AND-ROUTE.md`, `SUBMISSION.md`, `BENCHMARK.md`,
`CONTENT-REQUIREMENTS.md`, `PROFILE.md`, `PRICING-AND-FEATURES.md`, `CATEGORY-AND-COMPARISONS.md`,
`REVIEWS-AND-OPERATIONS.md`, `CHECKLIST.md`, `analytics.md`, and shared positioning.

## Workflow

### 1. Identify and research

Ask for the canonical product URL, vendor, product category, buyer/job, official pricing page, current SaaSworthy
URL, approved logo/screenshots, real integrations, and owner authority. Browse product/pricing/docs/support/legal
sources, then search exact and variant product/vendor names on SaaSworthy. Choose `NEW`, `CLAIM/UPDATE`,
`CORRECT/DUPLICATE`, or `NOT READY`.

### 2. Benchmark exactly five peers

Record category definition/order and inspect exactly five relevant profiles, their official sites, and a comparison.
Capture the public pattern: Overview, Features, Pricing, Alternatives, Reviews, FAQ, Contact, Customers,
Integrations, CTA, categories, screenshots, plans, free trial/freemium status, visible freshness/disclaimer, and
buyer objections. Classify every observed surface `LIVE-FORM/REQUIRED`, `CATEGORY BASELINE`, `RECOMMENDED`,
`PLATFORM/COMMUNITY-GENERATED`, `PAID/PROGRAM-GATED`, or `NOT APPLICABLE`.

### 3. Build the paste-ready listing

Capture the exact current list/claim/vendor-portal fields and constraints in `SUBMISSION.md`. Prepare source-backed:

- product/vendor identity, canonical URL, concise buyer-job statement, full overview, approved CTA
- accurate category/subcategory and target company size/industry when requested
- platforms/deployment, integrations, support/contact, documentation, security/compliance only when evidenced
- pricing options, free trial/freemium status, plan name, price, billing interval/unit, inclusions, official pricing
  URL, and checked date; explicitly flag values the marketplace must refresh
- features/specs mapped to the official product, screenshots/video with captions, source/rights, freshness, and
  secret/PII review
- genuine alternatives and factual differences; no keyword-stuffed competitor capture

Write three evidence-backed angles (buyer job, workflow/technical, and proof/reassurance), recommend one, and make
the field-by-field source ledger. Public pages may expose pricing insights, scores, reviews, comparisons, FAQs and
generated category context; never claim these are vendor inputs or reproduce stale data.

### 4. Reviews, operations, and handoff

If reviews are available, use a neutral post-use invitation with universal eligibility, a full eligible cohort,
delay, frequency cap, reminder, suppression, privacy basis, and audit owner. Ask for an honest experience, no rating
or sentiment. Respond publicly only as an identified vendor with facts and no PII. Separate paid/vendor-program
offers from organic listing status and verify written scope/cost before suggesting one.

Measure listing/category/comparison referrals, CTA conversion, pricing-page freshness, profile completeness, review
volume/recency, and response SLA with privacy-safe UTMs. Revalidate after any material pricing, feature, product,
brand, security, integration, or support change. Final verdict: `READY TO SUBMIT`, `READY TO CLAIM/UPDATE`,
`LIVE — OPERATIONS ACTIVE`, or `NOT READY — BLOCKERS REMAIN`.
