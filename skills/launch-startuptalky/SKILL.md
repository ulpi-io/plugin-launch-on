# Launch on StartupTalky

## Goal

Create `.ulpi/launch/startuptalky/` with `ROUTE-AND-FIT.md`, `BENCHMARK.md`, `STORY-PACKET.md`,
`SOURCE-REGISTER.md`, `FORM-CAPTURE.md`, `COMMERCIAL-DISCLOSURE.md`, `CORRECTIONS-RUNBOOK.md`, `analytics.md`, and
shared positioning.

## Workflow

### 1. Choose the legitimate story route

Collect the story/event, product/company facts, India/sector relevance, primary sources, founder authority, approved
metrics, customer permissions, media, and budget. Return `READY TO PITCH`, `READY TO REVIEW COMMERCIAL OPTION`, or
`NOT NEWSWORTHY`.

### 2. Benchmark exactly five peers

Compare five current relevant articles for headline, category, author/date, factual proof, business-model/founder arc,
reader value, citations, disclosure, and correction route. Extract patterns without copying or asserting entitlement.

### 3. Prepare a source-governed package

Create concise pitch options, one-sentence relevance, factual summary, source links, approved quotes, founder/company
bio, milestone evidence, media, category fit, disclosure, and correction contact. Capture every live Get Featured
field, required marker, upload rule, consent, and price after approval.

### 4. Submit and operate with approval

Present exact outreach/form values, sources, disclosures, terms, and owner. After publication, verify title, facts,
links, labels, category, and correction path; measure qualified referrals, engagement, response/publication status,
accuracy corrections, and paid spend separately. End `LIVE — STARTUPTALKY RUNBOOK ACTIVE`.
