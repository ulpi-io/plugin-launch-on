# Launch on Seedtable

## Goal

Create `.ulpi/launch/seedtable/` with `ROUTE-AND-ELIGIBILITY.md`, `BENCHMARK.md`, `PROFILE-EVIDENCE-REGISTER.md`,
`COMPANY-UPDATE-PACKET.md`, `FUNDING-UPDATE-PACKET.md`, `FORM-CAPTURE.md`, `CORRECTION-LOG.md`, `analytics.md`, and
positioning.

## Workflow

### 1. Choose the data route

Return `NEW COMPANY TIP READY`, `EXISTING PROFILE CORRECTION READY`, `FUNDING UPDATE READY`, `SEEDTABLE DIRECT OPTION
TO REVIEW`, or `NOT YET EVIDENCE-READY`. Verify the entity's canonical name/domain, legal/company identity, category,
geography, stage, and whether a profile already exists before proposing a new record.

### 2. Benchmark exactly five profiles

Use `$browse` on five comparable current Seedtable profiles and the closest public category/ranking. Compare only
observable facts: identity, category, geography, stage, disclosed funding, people/investors, source treatment,
recency, score/rank, and whether a paywall hides profile detail. Derive a factual taxonomy and evidence standard; do
not imitate wording or attempt to influence ranking.

### 3. Build a verification-ready packet

Prepare a concise plain-language submission plus an evidence register. Include canonical name, aliases, official
domain, logo/brand permission if requested, founding date, HQ and operating geography, category, product description,
founders, launch status, and sources. For a funding update, include round type/date, amount/currency, investors,
source provenance, whether the information is announced, and a correction owner. Label each fact `FIRST-PARTY`,
`INDEPENDENT`, `INFERRED`, or `UNKNOWN`.

### 4. Handle Seedtable Direct deliberately

Use the Direct option only for a representative-authorized company or funding update and only after reconfirming live
requirements. Direct is a source/provenance label, not a guarantee of coverage; Seedtable states it still verifies
submissions and may corroborate them independently. Keep a redacted public-safe version and an internal evidence set.

### 5. Submit with approval and maintain accuracy

Show the exact current form or email route, field-to-source map, final copy, links, public/private boundary, and any
account/paid-feature effect for approval. After authorized submission, save the receipt, monitor the public record,
request correction when evidence changes, and measure profile accuracy, inclusion status, correction turnaround, and
qualified referrals—never rank movement as a promised outcome. End `LIVE — SEEDTABLE RUNBOOK ACTIVE`.
