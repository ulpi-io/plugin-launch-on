1. DISTINCT LINKEDIN SURFACE. LinkedIn's public Products page on July 23, 2026 advertised 30,000 products, categories,
   user endorsements/testimonials, ratings/reviews, leads, and `Publish a product`. It is separate from Company Pages
   and `$launch-linkedin` post campaigns. Verify live availability and eligibility before promising publication.
2. ACCOUNT-GATED ACTIONS NEED APPROVAL. Never create/login, publish/claim/edit a product, change associated company
   admins, solicit endorsements, reply publicly, or export/respond to leads without explicit user approval. After
   authorization, capture all live fields, limits, required assets, category rules, and review controls in order.
3. BENCHMARK EXACTLY FIVE LIVE PRODUCTS WITH $browse. Capture URL, category, product promise, associated company,
   proof, screenshots, features, use cases, pricing cue, rating/recommendation display, CTA, and buyer objections. Do
   not copy wording or fabricate endorsements, users, ratings, customers, awards, integrations, or rankings.
4. PRODUCT FACTS MUST MATCH THE PRODUCT. Require official name/domain, owning company authorization, buyer, capabilities,
   availability, pricing/trial, integrations, support, security/compliance proof, logo/media, and terms. Do not
   treat a Company Page as evidence that a product is eligible or that LinkedIn will surface it.
5. GOVERN LEADS AND USER DATA. Name a lead owner, response SLA, consent/privacy basis, CRM access control, retention,
   delete/escalation path, and review-response owner. No review incentives conditioned on positive sentiment.
</EXTREMELY-IMPORTANT>

# Launch on LinkedIn Product Pages

## Goal

Create `.ulpi/launch/linkedin-product-page/` with `ROUTE-AND-ELIGIBILITY.md`, `BENCHMARK.md`,
`PRODUCT-PACKAGE.md`, `RATINGS-AND-RECOMMENDATIONS.md`, `LEAD-OPERATIONS.md`, `GOVERNANCE.md`, `CHECKLIST.md`,
`analytics.md`, and shared positioning.

## Workflow

### 1. Resolve eligibility

Ask for product URL/name, company-page authority, buyer/problem/outcome, category, features, media, availability,
pricing, support, review candidates, lead owner, privacy/CRM owner, existing Product Page, and approved proof. Browse
the live products/publish route. Return `READY TO PUBLISH`, `READY TO CLAIM/UPDATE`, or `NOT ELIGIBLE — NO CURRENT
ROUTE`.

### 2. Benchmark five current Product Pages

Select five peers by category, buyer, maturity, and deployment. Record public categories, positioning, associated
company, feature/use-case framing, assets, social proof, ratings/recommendations, CTA, and lead path. Label observed
facts vs inference; do not duplicate competitor content.

### 3. Build exact field-ready content

After authorized live form observation, prepare every field: official product/company, product URL, concise/expanded
description, categories, buyer/use cases, features/integrations, deployment, pricing/trial, logo/screenshots/video,
support, approved proof, product CTA, and lead disclosure. Build a truthful review request plus response workflow.

### 4. Publish and operate

Present exact fields, assets, associated company, lead routing, data handling, and review outreach for approval before
action. Track approval, page visibility, referral/leads, response SLA, conversion, user recommendations, review
themes, product accuracy, and access changes. End `LIVE — LINKEDIN PRODUCT PAGE GOVERNED`.
