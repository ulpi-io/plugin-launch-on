## Community integrity

AlternativeTo permits sharing a product page socially, but users should like or comment because they genuinely
find it useful. Never:

- incentivize likes, votes, comments, or reviews with discounts, gifts, access, or payment
- pressure employees, customers, friends, or communities to like it
- create fake/duplicate accounts
- hire or use a service to generate engagement
- script or ghostwrite user reviews/comments
- use a personal profile as a product advertisement
- publish spammy public Lists to promote an unlisted or unrelated app
- attack competitors or coordinate negative alternative votes

Manipulation may lower rank or remove the app from the front page. Ask for honest evaluation, not support.

## Announcement templates

### Published-page announcement

~~~text
[Product] is now listed on AlternativeTo. If you have used it for [primary task], you can review the listing
and share an honest opinion about whether it is a useful alternative: [AlternativeTo profile URL]
~~~

### Factual correction request

~~~text
The AlternativeTo page for [Product] is available here: [profile URL]. If you notice an inaccurate platform,
license, feature, or alternative association, please use the site's contribution tools or tell the product
team so the record can be corrected.
~~~

Do not add `please like`, a reward, a target count, or a deadline.

## Comments and reviews

AlternativeTo uses one page for comments and reviews: a star rating makes it a review; without a rating it is
a comment. Comments/reviews require admin approval before they appear.

Users must write from their own experience in their own words. The product team may answer factual questions
or correct misinformation respectfully, but must not dispute opinions, expose account details, or request a
rating change. Report suspicious activity to support rather than retaliating.

## Admin review and ownership

The official FAQ says app approval normally takes a couple of days to one week. Admins may deny entries at
their discretion even when similar apps exist.

Track separately:

- account eligible
- submitted
- pending moderation
- changes requested
- approved
- public URL verified
- developer admin rights granted
- edits approved
- alternative associations approved

For ownership, email `support@alternativeto.net` with account username, app name/page, and proof. A domain email
is suitable proof. Name and official-website edits require admin approval even for developer accounts.

## Ranking expectations

AlternativeTo says likes are an important rank input but uses other undisclosed factors. Organic quality
matters. Do not promise rank, front-page placement, traffic, or approval. Optimize controllable inputs:

- accurate and complete profile
- strong main-task fit
- legitimate alternative associations
- current media, platforms, pricing, and license
- organic user interest and useful discussion
- timely factual corrections

## Attribution

AlternativeTo generally rejects UTM parameters in Official Website URLs because users expect clean links.
Track referrals using:

- server/analytics referrer containing `alternativeto.net`
- landing sessions and engagement
- signup, activation, demo, purchase, and assisted conversion events
- self-reported discovery source when appropriate

Do not fingerprint users or add prohibited redirect tracking. Document referrer limitations from privacy tools,
browsers, apps, and HTTPS behavior.

## 30/60/90-day operations

### During seven-day account aging

- verify email and preserve account creation evidence
- complete eligibility and duplicate research
- prepare top-five benchmark and alternative map
- finish descriptions, taxonomy, platforms, license, icon, and screenshots
- do not create promotional profile activity to work around the gate

### Days 0–30 after eligibility

- submit after user review/authorization
- monitor moderation and answer admin questions
- verify public profile and request developer admin rights
- validate clean-link referrer attribution
- suggest only strongest alternative associations

### Days 31–60

- review approved associations, tags/features, screenshots, and comments
- correct factual inaccuracies through contribution tools
- share a neutral published-page announcement once
- evaluate qualified referral behavior

### Days 61–90

- add only newly justified associations/features
- analyze conversion and recurring buyer questions
- refresh copy/media when the product changes
- document support/admin outcomes

### Quarterly

Recheck official URL, availability, English support, creator, platforms, pricing/license, source license,
descriptions, types/tags/features, icon/screenshots/video, alternatives, admin rights, comments, and analytics.
