# Launch on SoftwareSuggest

## Outputs

Create `.ulpi/launch/softwaresuggest/` with `ROUTE-AND-DUPLICATES.md`, `SUBMISSION.md`, `BENCHMARK.md`,
`CONTENT-REQUIREMENTS.md`, `PROFILE.md`, `CATEGORIES-AND-COMPARISONS.md`, `REVIEWS-AND-AWARDS.md`,
`OPERATIONS.md`, `CHECKLIST.md`, `analytics.md`, and shared positioning.

## 1. Ground the listing route

Ask for canonical product/company URLs, primary buyer/job, target category, pricing/trial/demo URL, supported
platforms/deployment, integrations/API, company facts, approved media, existing SoftwareSuggest URL, and owner.
Search duplicates. Produce `NEW`, `CLAIM/UPDATE`, `CORRECTION/MERGE`, `AWARD/PROGRAM-ONLY`, or `NOT READY`.

Browse product, docs, pricing, integrations, support/security/legal pages and source every claim. Open Add a Product/
vendor flow only to capture live fields and constraints; record each `READY TO PASTE`, `SELECT`, `UPLOAD`, `NEEDS
OWNER`, `NOT APPLICABLE`, or `BLOCKED`.

## 2. Benchmark five peers and derive requirements

Capture category definition, filters/order, visible review counts, product cards, comparison links, awards, and date.
Inspect exactly five comparable profiles, official sites, and a comparison. Capture listing identity/CTA, description,
pricing/trial, feature set, images/video, reviews/roles, alternatives, category taxonomies, support, and buyer
questions. Classify every surface as `LIVE-FORM`, `CATEGORY BASELINE`, `RECOMMENDED`,
`PLATFORM/COMMUNITY-GENERATED`, `PROGRAM/PAID`, or `NOT APPLICABLE`.

## 3. Prepare all profile content

Write evidence-backed values for product/vendor identity; concise and full buyer-job description; primary/secondary
categories; feature/use-case mapping; integrations/API; deployment and native platforms; target users/industries;
pricing model, plan/unit/currency/frequency, trial/free-version state, official URL and checked date; support/training;
approved logo/screenshots/video with captions, rights, freshness and secret/PII check; genuine competitors and factual
differences; and source-backed FAQs. Draft buyer outcome, workflow/technical, and proof/reassurance angles, then
recommend one that differs factually from the five peers.

## 4. Reviews, awards, comparisons, and measurement

Use a neutral post-use review invitation for a universal eligible cohort with event/delay, cap, reminder, suppression,
privacy basis, and audit owner. Request an honest experience, never a rating or positive result. State vendor
affiliation in responses, protect PII, and correct with sources. Treat award applications, brand services, price
estimator, lead programs, ranking and AI-discovery promotion as separate current programs; verify entitlement,
price, labels, duration, and metrics before participation.

Measure category/profile/comparison referral, CTA-to-demo/trial/docs conversion, review recency, response SLA,
profile data freshness, program spend, and downstream qualified activation with privacy-safe UTMs. Final verdict:
`READY TO SUBMIT`, `READY TO CLAIM/UPDATE`, `LIVE — OPERATIONS ACTIVE`, or `NOT READY — BLOCKERS REMAIN`.
