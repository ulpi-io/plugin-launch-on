# Launch on StartupBase

## Goal

Create `.ulpi/launch/startupbase/` with `ROUTE-AND-READINESS.md`, `BENCHMARK.md`, `LAUNCH-PACKET.md`,
`TOPIC-PLAN.md`, `COMMERCIAL-DECISION.md`, `FORM-CAPTURE.md`, `COMMUNITY-RUNBOOK.md`, `analytics.md`, and positioning.

## Workflow

### 1. Select the launch route

Return `FREE QUEUE READY`, `PREMIUM LAUNCH OPTION TO REVIEW`, `AD/REVIEW OPTION TO REVIEW`, or `NOT LAUNCH-READY`.
Confirm product readiness, canonical domain, appropriate topics, date/support owner, and willingness to meet any current
badge requirement.

### 2. Benchmark exactly five product pages

Use `$browse` to compare five live comparable listings and their topic context. Extract observable positioning, topic
selection, card/page assets, launch timing, interaction context, Premium/sponsor labelling, and CTA. Build original,
evidence-based launch copy and community feedback prompts.

### 3. Build the product and topic package

Prepare name, domain, concise promise, customer/problem/solution, 1–3 relevant topics, access/pricing, logo/screenshots
or demo, team identity, public support/correction URL, asset rights, sources, and UTMs. Write a genuine launch-day
response plan that requests candid feedback and forbids vote manipulation.

### 4. Capture account, purchase, and ad fields

After user-authorized sign-in, capture every product, topic, date, media, verification, payment, review, and publish
field/limit. For Premium/ads/review/newsletter, record exact cost/tax, duration/placement, editorial vs sponsored
labelling, refund, receipt owner, and all deliverables. Compare live terms with the observed figures.

### 5. Publish with approval and operate

Present form preview, source map, date/queue, badge state, plan, ad/editorial disclosure, and community plan for
approval. After authorized launch, verify public page/labels/links/UTMs, correct facts, and measure qualified referrals,
feedback, activation, conversion, and spend. End `LIVE — STARTUPBASE RUNBOOK ACTIVE`.
