# Launch on REVIEWS.io

## Goal

Create `.ulpi/launch/reviews-io/` with `FIT-AND-ROUTE.md`, `BENCHMARK.md`, `PROFILE-AND-WIDGET-PACKET.md`,
`REVIEW-INVITATION-PROGRAM.md`, `INTEGRATION-AND-DATA-MAP.md`, `ACCOUNT-FORM-CAPTURE.md`,
`RESPONSE-AND-ESCALATION.md`, `analytics.md`, and positioning.

## Workflow

### 1. Confirm fit and choose scope

Return `REVIEW PROGRAM READY`, `DATA/CONSENT FIRST`, `COMMERCIAL REVIEW NEEDED`, or `NOT READY`. Decide whether the
real need is company reviews, product reviews, UGC, surveys, reputation management, or a combination. Confirm a
completed-customer event, lawful data source, neutral invite timing, support owner, privacy basis, and remediation
path before any invitation.

### 2. Benchmark exactly five customer stories

Use `$browse` to review exactly five current, relevant customer stories. Extract factual collection and display
patterns, not outcome promises. Build a differentiated plan based on the company’s actual purchase/support journey.

### 3. Prepare the profile, data, and invitation packet

Create sources for company/product identity, domains, categories, logo, support/returns/privacy links, review
attributes, approved widget locations, integration system of record, consent/retention, invitation audience/event,
neutral copy, frequency caps, suppression/opt-out, incentive disclosure, review moderation, and escalation owner.
Do not expose personal data in public responses or UGC without suitable permission.

### 4. Capture current dashboard and commercial controls

After authorization, record onboarding/profile fields; trial and plan prices; payment, renewal and cancellation;
company/product review schemas; invite flows/templates; UGC/media rights; integrations; API; widgets; Google-related
settings; moderation; analytics/export; and reviewer-data controls. Map every selection to approved sources and show
the implementation preview before activation.

### 5. Activate and operate with integrity

After approval, verify the live destination, invite trigger, unsubscribe/suppression, widget rendering, disclosure,
and privacy handling. Monitor deliverability, coverage, response time, support resolutions, review themes, and
qualified conversion without claiming causation. Address actual policy violations through the platform process, retain
an audit trail, and never remove criticism merely because it is negative. End `LIVE — REVIEWS.IO TRUST RUNBOOK ACTIVE`.
