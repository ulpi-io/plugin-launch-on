1. APPSUMO IS A CURATED DEAL CHANNEL, NOT A DIRECTORY. The July 23, 2026 surface promoted limited-time software deals,
   AppSumo Select, List your own product, and a Partner Portal. Never promise acceptance, placement, sales volume,
   editorial support, or a permanent listing. Evaluate a proposal as a commercial partnership.
2. USE $browse AND BENCHMARK EXACTLY FIVE LIVE DEALS. Inspect the current partner/listing route and five relevant
   deals. Capture offer structure, redemption rules, tier/stacking cues, expiration, refunds, support/community
   expectations, product positioning, proof, objections, and CTA. Use $browse-stealth/Camoufox if challenged.
3. MODEL THE UNIT ECONOMICS BEFORE APPLYING. Require approved offer price, partner revenue share/fees, tax/payout
   terms, refund exposure, redemption rate, infra/support marginal cost, lifetime access obligations, upgrade path,
   roadmap, capacity, and break-even/worst-case scenarios. Stop if the company cannot honor the deal.
4. NO MISREPRESENTATION OR REVIEW MANIPULATION. Never invent normal price, discount, lifetime access, included limits,
   roadmap, integrations, users, traction, reviews, testimonial, or scarcity. Do not solicit or incentivize positive
   reviews, suppress refunds, or change essential deal terms after purchase without a legitimate remedy.
5. HUMAN APPROVAL REQUIRED. Do not submit an application, sign terms, create a deal, set prices, change product
   entitlements, reply publicly, process refunds, or access partner systems without explicit user approval.
</EXTREMELY-IMPORTANT>

# Launch on AppSumo

## Goal

Create `.ulpi/launch/appsumo/` with `ROUTE-AND-FIT.md`, `BENCHMARK.md`, `UNIT-ECONOMICS.md`, `DEAL-PACKAGE.md`,
`ENTITLEMENTS-AND-SUPPORT.md`, `CAMPAIGN-RUNBOOK.md`, `CHECKLIST.md`, `analytics.md`, and shared positioning.

## Workflow

### 1. Determine commercial fit

Ask for product maturity, current pricing, approved discounted offer, entitlement architecture, delivery cost,
support/onboarding owner, refund policy, legal/tax owner, runway/capacity, upgrade plan, existing AppSumo URL, and
commercial target. Browse current partner routes. Return `READY TO APPLY`, `READY FOR ECONOMICS REMEDIATION`, or
`NOT A FIT FOR THIS DEAL CHANNEL`.

### 2. Benchmark five live deals

Select five comparable products by buyer, maturity, model, and deal type. Record observed price/offer language,
tiers, included limits, product proof, FAQ/objections, review themes, support response, expiry, and post-deal CTA.
Separate facts from assumptions; do not copy offer structure blindly.

### 3. Build an application and deal packet

Prepare product/company overview, customer/problem/outcome, official URLs/media, live demo, differentiation,
pricing history, proposed deal/tier/limits, entitlement/redemption design, capacity plan, onboarding, support SLA,
FAQ, security/privacy, roadmap boundary, review response policy, and sources. Produce a unit-economics model for base,
high-redemption, high-refund, and support-spike cases.

### 4. Run the campaign safely

Before approval, show partner-form answers, terms, commercial model, assets, deal copy, entitlement changes, and
reply owner. Track application state, approval, sales, redemption, activation, support, refunds, review themes,
upgrade conversion, cohort retention, payout, and margin. Keep buyer terms and service quality truthful through and
after the campaign. End `LIVE — APPSUMO DEAL OPERATING`.
