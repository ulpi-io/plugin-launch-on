# Launch on Silicon Canals

## Workflow

### 1. Determine whether a story is defensible

Collect news hook, public-interest relevance, official source documents, attributable facts, company/founder authority,
embargo, permissions, jurisdiction, and correction owner. Return `READY TO PITCH`, `NEEDS PRIMARY EVIDENCE`, or `NOT A FIT`.

### 2. Benchmark exactly five stories

Record section, headline, author/date, claim/evidence pattern, narrative, geographic context, public sources, and correction posture. Do not copy prose or imply inclusion is available for purchase.

### 3. Build an editor-ready source packet

Prepare a concise pitch, factual summary, primary links, approved quote, media, confidential-material boundaries,
disclosure, and correction contact. Separate claimed facts from interpretation and label uncertainty.

### 4. Contact and measure only with approval

Present exact outreach, sources, permission status, privacy/embargo constraints, and owner. Verify published facts,
links, labels, and corrections; track response, publication, qualified referral, accuracy corrections, and source quality. End `LIVE — SILICON CANALS RUNBOOK ACTIVE`.
