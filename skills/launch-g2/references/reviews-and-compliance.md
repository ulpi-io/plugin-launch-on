# Authentic G2 review program

Verified against official G2 sources on 2026-07-23. Recheck G2's Community Guidelines before every campaign.

## Reviewer eligibility

Invite only people with genuine first-hand experience:

- current product users
- former users whose experience is still relevant
- trial or evaluation users with enough experience to provide useful detail
- business partners or resellers when their relationship and perspective are accurate

Do not invite:

- employees of the product's vendor
- employees of a direct competitor
- anyone who has not used or evaluated the product
- a person asked to submit another person's experience
- fabricated identities, purchased reviewers, or review exchanges

G2 may validate reviewers through an up-to-date LinkedIn identity, a business email, or other authenticity
signals. A screenshot showing actual product use may support a Current User label. Do not tell a reviewer to
expose confidential information.

## Non-negotiable collection rules

Never:

- review-gate by surveying sentiment first and inviting only promoters
- exclude accounts likely to be critical
- ask for a five-star or positive review
- provide suggested answers, scripts, or copied review text
- submit a review on a customer's behalf
- pressure a reviewer to edit or remove criticism
- identify or contact an anonymous reviewer to influence them
- offer an undisclosed incentive outside G2
- condition any incentive, refund, discount, credit, renewal, or service on review sentiment
- reuse reviews copied from another platform, even when the same reviewer wrote them

Invite a representative cohort using neutral language. Sampling may be operationally segmented by product,
role, region, or lifecycle only when every sentiment has an equal chance to participate.

## Incentives

G2's current Community Guidelines cap review incentives at USD 100. Incentives:

- reward an approved honest review, not a positive opinion
- must be disclosed to G2
- result in an Incentivized label when applicable
- should run through G2's campaign mechanisms or a process explicitly approved by G2

G2 supports incentivized and non-incentivized self-service campaigns. Do not assume campaign or billing
features exist on the user's plan; verify in my.G2. A credit card may be required for campaign overages.

## Invitation templates

### Initial invitation

Subject: Share your honest experience with PRODUCT on G2

Hi FIRST_NAME,

You've used PRODUCT for WORKFLOW, and we'd value an honest review of your experience on G2—what works, what
doesn't, and who you think it fits. Your feedback helps buyers make a better decision and helps our team
improve.

REVIEW_LINK

G2 independently moderates reviews. Please write in your own words and don't include confidential customer
or company information.

Thank you,
SENDER

### Reminder

Subject: Reminder: honest feedback on PRODUCT

Hi FIRST_NAME,

If you have enough experience with PRODUCT, here is the G2 review link again:

REVIEW_LINK

All ratings and opinions are welcome. If now is not a good time, no action is needed.

Thank you,
SENDER

### In-product prompt

Used PRODUCT enough to share an informed opinion? Leave an honest G2 review. All feedback is welcome.

Do not trigger the prompt based on a positive NPS/CSAT answer. Use neutral milestones such as sustained use,
completed onboarding, or a representative time-based sample.

## Campaign design

### Submission-form reviewer contacts

The Add your product form currently allows up to three customer emails for G2 outreach. Apply the same
representative-audience and reviewer-eligibility rules as any campaign. Confirm an appropriate privacy/legal
basis before sharing. Enter raw emails directly into G2 or an approved secure workflow; do not persist them
in .ulpi launch artifacts.

### Audience table

| Cohort | Eligibility basis | Count | Segment | Role/use case | Invite date | Incentive | Owner |
|---|---|---:|---|---|---|---|---|

The mix should reflect the actual customer base. Track small-business, mid-market, enterprise, roles, use
cases, tenure, geography, and category-relevant workflows without manufacturing a desired rating.

### Cadence

1. Initial cohort: a broad representative sample large enough to learn where moderation or messaging fails.
2. Follow-up cohort: remaining eligible users, adjusted only for operational issues—not sentiment.
3. Always-on: neutral invitations at valid lifecycle moments.
4. Quarterly refresh: invite new eligible users and monitor decaying review coverage.
5. Review updates: let G2's update requests and genuine product changes refresh older feedback.

Recent, detailed, current-user, and non-incentivized reviews can receive more algorithmic weight, but never
optimize those factors through coercion or selective collection.

## Reviewer preparation without coaching

You may explain:

- G2 verifies identity and manually moderates submissions
- reviews should be original, specific, and based on first-hand use
- reviewers should discuss both strengths and limitations honestly
- confidential or personal information should be omitted
- approval can take up to roughly three business days

Do not tell reviewers what rating, pros, cons, category, wording, or recommendation score to choose.

## Moderation operations

Track:

- invited
- clicked
- started
- submitted
- pending
- approved
- rejected/action required
- incentivized and reward state
- category attribution
- reviewer segment
- published date
- response status

Common rejection causes include unverifiable identity, vendor/competitor employment, copied text, insufficient
detail, unclear content, and duplicate reviews. Help users understand process requirements, but never rewrite
their reviews.

## Public response playbook

Respond to both positive and negative reviews.

### Positive

1. Thank the reviewer.
2. Reference a specific workflow they mentioned.
3. Reinforce a useful resource or next step.
4. Avoid promotional boilerplate.

### Critical

1. Acknowledge the experience without arguing.
2. Restate the issue accurately.
3. Explain what is known and what the team can do.
4. Link a public fix/resource when genuinely relevant.
5. Invite normal support contact without asking the reviewer to change the review.

Set a response SLA, normally two business days. Escalate security, privacy, outage, billing, and legal issues
through the company's existing incident paths before posting specifics.

Report a review only when there is concrete evidence of a guideline violation. G2 does not remove valid
negative reviews merely because a seller disputes them.

## Compliance scan

Reject any campaign containing:

- positive, five-star, good review, boost our rating, help us rank
- incentives mentioned without G2 disclosure
- a hidden promoter-only audience
- employees or direct competitors
- ghostwritten talking points or review text
- pressure to edit, delete, or retract
- mass outreach to people who have not used the product
