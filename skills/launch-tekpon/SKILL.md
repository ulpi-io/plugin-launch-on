# Launch on Tekpon

## Goal

Create `.ulpi/launch/tekpon/` with `ROUTE.md`, `BENCHMARK.md`, `PROFILE-PACKAGE.md`,
`SCORE-DEALS-AND-PROCUREMENT.md`, `MEDIA-AND-COMPARISONS.md`, `REVIEW-OPERATIONS.md`, `CHECKLIST.md`,
`analytics.md`, and shared positioning.

## Workflow

### 1. Resolve category and commercial route

Ask for product URL/name, buyer/problem/outcome, category, features, integrations, pricing, actual discount/deal,
supported markets, media, support, approved proof, review candidates, existing Tekpon URL, and budget. Browse the live
route and return `READY TO CREATE`, `READY TO CLAIM/UPDATE`, `READY TO EVALUATE DEAL`, or `BLOCKED`.

### 2. Benchmark five live peers

Choose five comparable products by buyer, category, maturity, price, and deployment. Record observed positioning,
category tags, score/review labels, pricing/deal visibility, visual pattern, social proof, comparison surfaces, and
buyer CTA. Mark `LIVE`, `PLATFORM-GENERATED`, `PAID`, `EDITORIAL`, `INFERRED`, or `UNKNOWN`.

### 3. Build the field-ready package

After authorized form observation, create exact values for product/vendor identity, concise/expanded description,
categories, use cases, features, integrations, deployment, pricing and terms, real deal conditions, logo/screenshots
with outcome captions, video, documentation/support, proof, competitor context, and review outreach. Maintain a source
ledger and a separate deal approval sheet.

### 4. Launch and measure

Show exact fields, assets, price/deal terms, review outreach, paid choice, score claims, and tracking URL for approval.
Track listing status, profile/category/comparison visits, referrals, conversion, deal redemption, qualified leads,
review health, support load, procurement introductions, and paid spend separately. End `LIVE — TEKPON PROFILE
OPERATING`.
