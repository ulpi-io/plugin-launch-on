## 1. Authentic reviews

Use a current SourceForge-generated review link or supported vendor workflow. Define an eligible cohort with a
real product experience, a neutral send event, consistent delay, frequency cap, reminder policy, suppression,
privacy basis, and owner. Invite the whole eligible cohort under the same rule; never filter by NPS, support
outcome, plan, spend, geography, predicted sentiment, or personal relationship.

Templates must ask for an honest review, identify the product and reason for contact, use the official review
destination, offer no value, and not provide suggested review text. Owners, employees, contractors, close family,
competitors, and other conflicts must not review.

## 2. Incentivized program boundary

Default to no incentive. SourceForge's current Vendor Reviews Program has allowed eligible upgraded vendors to
run disclosed incentives under SourceForge-controlled conditions. Before any such campaign, verify the current
policy and plan. The July 23, 2026 policy snapshot included a fixed campaign cap, first-approved basis, equal
reward regardless sentiment, country/recipient restrictions, SourceForge labeling, no sweepstakes/raffles, and
limits of up to $50 for a nominal gift card (with no more than $25 recommended) or $25 nonmonetary value.

These numbers may change. Never independently offer or fulfill an incentive, never condition it on rating or
approval, and never include ineligible employees/public-sector/sanctioned recipients. Require privacy and legal
approval plus SourceForge program confirmation.

## 3. Responses

Respond promptly, originally, and professionally. Thank the reviewer, acknowledge specifics without exposing
PII, explain or remedy what is possible, route account-specific help privately, and close with ownership. Do not
argue, harass, threaten, disclose customer data, stuff keywords, or pressure anyone to revise/delete a review.
Escalate credible safety, security, legal, billing, or outage reports internally.

## 4. Plans and paid placement

Base claim/edit/manage capability has been advertised as free for vendors. Plus, Premium, Amplify, Precision
ABM, Enterprise, and entitlements can change. Current paid examples include additional category/competitor
promotion, enhanced review campaigns, visitor/buyer-intent data, downloadable assets, demos, custom cover,
analytics tags, advertising, and demand generation.

Build a live entitlement matrix: needed, available, limit, Free/Paid/Add-on, evidence URL, verified date. Keep
profile completeness, organic relevance, sponsored placement, badges, and paid leads as separate concepts.

## 5. Analytics and cadence

Use UTM-tagged first-party destinations where permitted. Measure SourceForge referral sessions, CTA and pricing
clicks, trials/demos/signups, conversions/revenue, project downloads by file/version/platform, update adoption,
review volume/recency, response time, support volume, and release incidents. Document attribution windows and
bot/internal exclusions.

Weekly: broken links/downloads, support and critical reviews, release/security alerts. Monthly: traffic,
conversion, downloads, copy/pricing/integration freshness, review operations. Quarterly: categories/features,
competitors, plan ROI, access, privacy, assets, license/notices, stale files, and disaster recovery.
