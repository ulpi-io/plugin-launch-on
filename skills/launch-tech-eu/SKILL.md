## Workflow

### 1. Resolve route and news fit

Return `PITCH READY`, `DATA CORRECTION READY`, `CONTRIBUTION READY`, `PAID OPTION TO REVIEW`, or `NOT NEWSWORTHY` from live routes and source proof.

### 2. Benchmark exactly five peers

Prepare the story hook, company/founder facts, primary sources, approved quote, assets, topic, disclosure, correction contact, and separately labelled commercial terms. Capture actual form fields only after approval.

Present exact route, fields, terms, cost, assets, and owner. Verify published facts/labels/links and track qualified referrals, source accuracy, coverage/data status, and spend separately. End `LIVE — TECH.EU RUNBOOK ACTIVE`.
