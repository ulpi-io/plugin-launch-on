# Launch on Tracxn

## Goal

Create `.ulpi/launch/tracxn/` with `ROUTE.md`, `BENCHMARK.md`, `COMPANY-PACKAGE.md`, `TAXONOMY-AND-SOURCES.md`,
`FINANCIAL-DATA-BOUNDARY.md`, `CORRECTION-RUNBOOK.md`, `CHECKLIST.md`, `analytics.md`, and shared positioning.

## Workflow

### 1. Resolve inclusion/correction route

Ask for official identity/domain, approved description, sector/business model, location/founding date, public team,
approved funding disclosure, existing record, authorized sources, profile owner, finance/legal owner, and correction
goal. Return `READY TO REQUEST LISTING`, `READY TO CORRECT`, or `BLOCKED` after browsing live route.

### 2. Benchmark five records

Choose five peers by taxonomy, stage, geography, and model. Record observed company/sector/funding/news context and
source cues. Keep platform-generated labels separate from vendor claims and do not infer private financial values.

### 3. Prepare exact source-backed facts

After authorized form capture, map every field to an approved public source: identity, URL, description, taxonomy,
location, founded date, public people, and eligible public funding/news. Produce a financial-data boundary and a
correction packet showing current value, requested value, citation, owner, and approval date.

### 4. Request and maintain

Present exact fields, sources, recipients, disclosure choices, and submitter for approval. Track request status,
accuracy, taxonomy, corrections, public citations, referral quality, and material-event updates. End `LIVE — TRACXN
RECORD GOVERNED`.
