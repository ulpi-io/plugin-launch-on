# TrustRadius Reviews, Awards, and Operations

## Authentic reviews

Invite real users under one consistent eligibility rule and experience point. Never segment by NPS, predicted
sentiment, support resolution, plan, spend, or relationship. Use neutral language and the current supported
TrustRadius workflow. Do not write, suggest, translate into a more positive tone, or submit reviewer answers.

Public TrustRadius reviews can be labeled `Incentivized`. Default to no incentive. If requested, use only a
current TrustRadius-managed and disclosed campaign after verifying plan, recipient/country eligibility, value,
tax/privacy rules, conflicts, and equal treatment regardless rating. Never run an independent incentive.

Responses should acknowledge specifics, protect PII, offer a private resolution path, and avoid arguments,
legal threats, boilerplate, or pressure to revise/delete.

## Awards and generated content

TrustRadius controls score, rating, category rank, Top Rated/Buyer's Choice/Trusted Seller/Customer Verified
badges, AI insights, buyer/installation data, and review summaries. Verify current official award methodology,
minimum review/recency/category requirements, eligibility window, and licensing rules. Record controllable
inputs but never promise an award or badge.

## Free and Premium

The July 23, 2026 claim page advertised profile building, videos/demos, features/integrations, pricing, security,
awards/badges, and CTAs. It separately promoted Premium capabilities including expanded brand narrative,
review-generation and refresh campaigns, custom questions, TrustQuotes/content licensing, reference management,
intent data, lead capture, competitive analytics/reporting, and integrations. Re-verify every entitlement live.

## Operations and analytics

Weekly: broken CTAs, critical reviews/responses, security or pricing changes. Monthly: completeness, referral and
conversion funnel, review coverage/recency, media and integration freshness. Quarterly: categories/features,
competitors, security evidence, access, plan ROI, privacy, award eligibility, and stale content.

Track category/profile exposure when available, Source=TrustRadius referral sessions, CTA/demo/trial/contact
conversion, review start/completion, score/count/recency (without promises), response time, award state, and
Premium leads/intent only when entitled. Use UTMs and first-party events; do not scrape reviewer identities.
