# Launch on BetaPage / PitchWall

## Goal

Create `.ulpi/launch/betapage/` with `LIVE-SURFACE-NOTE.md`, `ROUTE-AND-FIT.md`, `BENCHMARK.md`, `LAUNCH-PACKET.md`,
`COMMERCIAL-DECISION.md`, `FORM-CAPTURE.md`, `EDITORIAL-ADVERTISING-BOUNDARIES.md`, `analytics.md`, and positioning.

## Workflow

### 1. Verify the live platform and select route

Confirm the current redirect and return `FREE LISTING READY`, `PRO OPTION TO REVIEW`, `PREMIUM OPTION TO REVIEW`,
`AD/EDITORIAL OPTION TO REVIEW`, or `NOT READY`. Recheck the wait/date and select no commercial path until its full
terms and product-fit rationale are documented.

### 2. Benchmark exactly five peer pages

Use `$browse` to compare five current, relevant PitchWall product pages. Capture observable positioning, tag/category,
asset/CTA pattern, product access, and sponsor/featured labels. Write original, factual product copy with evidence.

### 3. Build the source-governed listing packet

Prepare canonical product/company name, URL, succinct benefit, target user, category/tags, pricing/access, logo/media
rights, public support contact, UTM destination, proof sources, and correction owner. Maintain a separate optional
advertising/guest-post pitch and clearly label any paid or sponsored relationship.

### 4. Capture plan and form details

After authorized plan progression, record exact fields/limits and commercial conditions: price, tax, wait/date, home
placement, newsletter/social treatment, link claim, payment/refund, review, disclosure, and confirmation. Highlight any
difference from the observed Free/Pro/Premium terms.

### 5. Submit with approval and operate

Present final form preview, sources, public/private boundary, selected plan, current payment terms, and editorial/ad
disclosures for approval. After authorized publication, verify listing, placements/labels, link/UTM, correct facts, and
measure qualified referrals, activation, conversion, and spend. End `LIVE — BETAPAGE/PITCHWALL RUNBOOK ACTIVE`.
