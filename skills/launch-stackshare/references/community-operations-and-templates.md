# StackShare community, operations, and handoff templates

## Ethical participation

Vendor representatives may keep factual information current and answer developer questions transparently. State the
relationship, link to primary evidence, avoid confidential details, and correct errors calmly. A community member’s
stack addition, follow, vote, pros/cons, discussion, or decision must be independent and truthful. Do not script,
reward, coordinate, or filter those actions.

## Submission checklist

```md
# StackShare submission checklist

- [ ] Correct route and duplicate search recorded
- [ ] Exactly five live peer profiles, official sites, and comparison evidence recorded
- [ ] Live create/edit fields and limits captured
- [ ] Product identity, category, features, integrations, alternatives, and CTA are source-backed
- [ ] Licensing, platforms, OSS status, and pricing claims are accurate
- [ ] Logo/media rights, captions, and secret/PII review complete
- [ ] Official claim authority and verification route confirmed
- [ ] No community-signal manipulation or false company-stack claim
- [ ] Human approval received before external action
```

## Metrics

| Metric | Method | Cadence | Guardrail |
|---|---|---|---|
| Profile/category referral | privacy-safe UTM and first-party event | weekly | do not identify users without a basis |
| CTA conversion | docs/trial/demo event | weekly | distinguish test traffic |
| Comparison referral | comparison URL UTM/referrer | monthly | do not engineer false alternative links |
| Claim/profile freshness | field evidence ledger | quarterly/release | update after material change |
| Community signal | observed stacks/follows/votes/discussions | monthly | descriptive only; never a target for manipulation |
```
