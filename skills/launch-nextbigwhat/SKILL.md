# Launch on NextBigWhat

## Goal

Create `.ulpi/launch/nextbigwhat/` with `ROUTE-AND-TERMS.md`, `BENCHMARK.md`, `LISTING-PACKET.md`,
`CHECKOUT-AND-FORM-CAPTURE.md`, `ASSET-CHECKLIST.md`, `COMMERCIAL-DISCLOSURE.md`, `FOLLOW-UP-RUNBOOK.md`,
`analytics.md`, and shared positioning.

## Workflow

### 1. Resolve product and package fit

Collect product URL, official name, target user/problem, approved outcome claim, pricing/access, media, launch date,
and budget. Browse the live route and return `READY TO REVIEW PACKAGE`, `NEEDS PRODUCT EVIDENCE`, or `NOT A FIT`.

### 2. Benchmark exactly five products

Record each peer's listing title, tagline, description structure, category, audience, screenshot treatment, CTA,
product link, and factual claim density. Derive original positioning without copying.

### 3. Build the post-payment listing packet

Prepare form-ready name, concise tagline, factual description, approved product link, product image, tracking URL,
category recommendation, access/pricing facts, support route, and correction contact. Keep package price, duration,
claimed visibility, tax/refund/cancellation, and approval owner in a separate commercial record.

### 4. Purchase, publish, and measure only with approval

Present exact package, current checkout/form fields, final assets, terms, spend, and owner for approval. After live,
verify title, link, image, description, visibility labels, and corrections. Track referral sessions, engaged visits,
qualified conversions, package cost, and source accuracy. End `LIVE — NEXTBIGWHAT RUNBOOK ACTIVE`.
