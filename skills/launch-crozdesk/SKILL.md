# Launch on Crozdesk

## Outputs

Create `.ulpi/launch/crozdesk/` with `APPLICATION-AND-CLAIM.md`, `SUBMISSION.md`, `BENCHMARK.md`,
`CONTENT-REQUIREMENTS.md`, `PROFILE.md`, `PRICING-AND-FEATURES.md`, `CATEGORY-AND-BUYER-FIT.md`,
`REVIEWS-AND-PROMOTION.md`, `CHECKLIST.md`, `analytics.md`, and shared positioning.

## 1. Ground identity and route

Ask for the canonical product URL, vendor, main buyer/job, intended Crozdesk category, pricing URL, supported
platforms, regional/language limits, official integrations, approved media, existing Crozdesk URL, and owner
authority. Search exact and variant product/vendor names. Select `NEW VENDOR APPLICATION`, `CLAIM EXISTING`,
`CORRECT/MERGE`, `OPTIMIZE`, or `NOT READY`.

Crozdesk’s current public materials say a vendor can apply for a free listing, receive vendor-portal access after
application review, complete the listing, and submit it for approval. Its current public claim guidance says an
unowned listing can be claimed subject to authority review. Capture all current application/claim fields and every
portal field in exact order; do not rely on older blog steps as immutable UI.

## 2. Research exactly five peers

Browse the live target category and record definition/order. Inspect exactly five comparable product profiles, each
official vendor site, plus a comparison/category page. Capture main/other categories, overview, score/rating/review
count as observed, pricing, plan names and units, trial/freemium, target/suitable users, native applications,
languages/regions, vendor details, feature list, integrations, FAQs, screenshots, alternatives/similar products,
review patterns, CTA, and last-modified data. Classify every field as `LIVE-FORM`, `CATEGORY BASELINE`,
`RECOMMENDED`, `PLATFORM/COMMUNITY-GENERATED`, `OPTIONAL PAID`, or `NOT APPLICABLE`.

## 3. Prepare the listing package

Produce paste-ready, evidence-linked answers for:

- vendor/product identity, canonical landing and conversion destination, concise buyer-job description and full overview
- main category and additional categories, category-fit rationale, buyer segments/industries/company sizes, use cases
- deployment/native applications, languages, geographic limits, support/training, API/integrations, and only proved
  security/compliance claims
- pricing type, free trial/freemium status, plan names, amount, currency, per-user/per-month/year unit, inclusions,
  official pricing URL, and verification date
- features selected only when genuinely available; screenshots/video/logo with approved source, rights, caption,
  dimensions, freshness, and PII/secret check
- genuine alternatives/similar products and factual differentiation; never comparison-keyword stuffing
- FAQ answers that are sourced and useful to buyers

Write three angles (buyer outcome, workflow/implementation, proof/reassurance) and choose the best source-backed
one. Put every live field, help text, requirement, limit, state, owner, and source in `SUBMISSION.md`.

## 4. Reviews, promotion, and measurement

Use a neutral, post-use review invitation to the full eligible cohort with a consistent event/delay, reminder and
suppression policy. Ask for an honest experience, not a rating. Identify vendor affiliation in public responses,
protect PII, correct factual errors with source links, and do not attack competitors.

Public Crozdesk material distinguishes the free listing from optional vendor-portal promotion, widgets, conversion
tracking, and referral campaigns. Confirm entitlement and current commercial terms in writing; do not imply paid
placement or editorial work is organic. Measure category/profile/comparison referral, CTA/demos, qualified referral
conversion, profile completeness, pricing freshness, review recency, and response SLA with consent-aware first-party
analytics.

## 5. Final validation

Confirm exactly-five live evidence, duplicate/claim route, current form fields, category and buyer fit, pricing/unit
accuracy, feature/media proof, no private data, fair-review plan, program separation, and human approval. End with
`READY TO APPLY`, `READY TO CLAIM/UPDATE`, `LIVE — OPERATIONS ACTIVE`, or `NOT READY — BLOCKERS REMAIN`.
