# Launch on Fazier

## Goal

Create `.ulpi/launch/fazier/` with `ROUTE-AND-READINESS.md`, `BENCHMARK.md`, `LAUNCH-PACKET.md`,
`COMMERCIAL-DECISION.md`, `FORM-CAPTURE.md`, `COMMUNITY-RUNBOOK.md`, `analytics.md`, and positioning.

## Workflow

### 1. Choose launch or advertising route

Return `BASIC READY`, `LITE OPTION TO REVIEW`, `PREMIUM/SUPER OPTION TO REVIEW`, `NEWSLETTER/AD OPTION TO REVIEW`, or
`NOT LAUNCH-READY`. Confirm product function, maker authority, category, launch support, and whether any badge/backlink
condition is feasible and approved.

### 2. Benchmark exactly five peers

Use `$browse` to inspect exactly five comparable public Fazier launches. Compare visible positioning, pricing label,
rank/score context, Premium/Sponsor disclosure, and CTA. Build a truthful differentiated product story and feedback
response plan—not a scoring playbook.

### 3. Build approved launch assets

Prepare canonical name/domain, concise benefit, customer/problem/solution, pricing/access label, category, screenshots/
demo, maker/support identity, source register, UTM URLs, and correction owner. Separate requested product facts from
paid-placement copy; mark sponsorship and advertiser relationships clearly.

### 4. Capture live form and terms

After explicit authorization, record all account/submit fields and current Basic/Lite/Premium/Super or ad terms. Include
cost, tax, queue/date, duration, homepage/platform/newsletter placement, badge/link treatment, refund, receipt owner,
and confirmation. Flag deviations from observed terms.

### 5. Publish with approval and operate

Present the form preview, sources, selected plan, badge/placement condition, commercial disclosure, and community plan
for approval. After authorized launch, verify page/labels/links/UTMs, reply transparently, correct errors, and measure
qualified referrals, feedback, activation, conversion, and spend. End `LIVE — FAZIER RUNBOOK ACTIVE`.
