# Launch on Springwise

## Goal

Create `.ulpi/launch/springwise/` with `ELIGIBILITY.md`, `BENCHMARK.md`, `FORM-PACKET.md`,
`IMPACT-EVIDENCE.md`, `NARRATIVE-ANSWERS.md`, `ASSET-AND-PERMISSIONS.md`, `SUBMISSION-CHECKLIST.md`,
`FOLLOW-UP-RUNBOOK.md`, `analytics.md`, and shared positioning.

## Workflow

### 1. Prove innovation and impact fit

Collect official product/company sources, owner/contact, company model, company size, locations, funding facts,
development stage, product availability, industries served, problem scale, technical/operating mechanism, deployment
proof, impact metrics, chosen impact area, SDGs, customer/partner permissions, and future roadmap. Browse live routes
and classify `READY TO SUBMIT`, `NEEDS EVIDENCE`, or `NOT A SPRINGWISE FIT`.

### 2. Benchmark exactly five profiles

Choose five comparable innovation/database or implementation profiles. Record title, category hierarchy, problem
framing, solution mechanism, implementation context, factual proof, impact framing, author/date, reader CTA, and
membership boundary. Derive a factual information architecture without copying editorial text.

### 3. Produce the exact form packet

Prepare form-ready values for every current field: contact, company name/size/site/model/location, funding stage and
amount, product name/link, development stage, available countries, industries, impact area, SDGs, and answers to:
problem significance; what the innovation is and how it works; benefits/impacts; where/how it is applied; and future
opportunity. Every claim needs a source, confidence label, and approval owner.

### 4. Submit, verify, and operate with approval

Before submission, present the final fields, sources, sensitive data, content permissions, CAPTCHA, terms, and owner
for approval. After a listing is live, verify title, profile URL, taxonomy, factual statements, links, images, access
labels, and corrections route. Track publication, referrals, qualified engagement, source accuracy, opportunities,
and any membership/commercial cost separately. End `LIVE — SPRINGWISE RUNBOOK ACTIVE`.
