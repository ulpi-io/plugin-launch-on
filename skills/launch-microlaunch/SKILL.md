# Launch on Microlaunch

## Goal

Create `.ulpi/launch/microlaunch/` with `ROUTE-AND-READINESS.md`, `BENCHMARK.md`, `LAUNCH-PACKET.md`,
`FEEDBACK-RUNBOOK.md`, `DEAL-PACKET.md`, `COMMERCIAL-DECISION.md`, `FORM-CAPTURE.md`, `analytics.md`, and positioning.

## Workflow

### 1. Choose the correct route

Return `REGULAR LAUNCH READY`, `PRO LAUNCH OPTION TO REVIEW`, `DEAL READY`, `PRODUCT REVIEW OPTION TO REVIEW`, or `NOT
LAUNCH-READY`. Confirm a live/testable product, category, approved maker identity, launch support owner, and whether a
deal/review has commercial and operational justification.

### 2. Benchmark exactly five peers

Use `$browse` on five comparable listings and their nearest leaderboard/category context. Compare observable positioning,
product type, maker treatment, scores/roasts/review counts, featured/deal labels, CTA, and public update cadence.
Produce a truthful value proposition and feedback hypotheses—not score manipulation tactics.

### 3. Build launch, feedback, and deal packets

Prepare canonical name/domain, customer/problem/solution, concise tagline, category/product type, product access,
pricing, logo/screenshots/demo, maker profile, support URL, source register, and UTM links. For deals, separately verify
discount, eligibility, redemption rules, end date, support, and legal/commercial approval. For feedback, invite candid
testing with no reward tied to sentiment.

### 4. Re-capture live account and commercial fields

After user-authorized access, record exact New Launch form fields and limits. For Pro/review/deal options, capture live
price/currency/tax, coverage/duration, terms/refund, placement, payment receipt owner, review process/independence, and
publication state. Present an explicit value/risk comparison.

### 5. Publish with approval and operate

Show the form preview, source map, selected path, product/deal assets, current commercial terms, and feedback plan for
approval. After authorized launch, verify card/labels/links/UTMs, engage transparently, correct factual errors, and
measure qualified referrals, tester activation, feedback themes, conversions, deal redemptions, and spend. End `LIVE —
MICROLAUNCH RUNBOOK ACTIVE`.
