# Launch on Dealroom

## Goal

Create `.ulpi/launch/dealroom/` with `ROUTE.md`, `BENCHMARK.md`, `COMPANY-PACKAGE.md`,
`PUBLIC-DATA-BOUNDARY.md`, `SIGNALS-AND-SOURCES.md`, `CORRECTION-RUNBOOK.md`, `CHECKLIST.md`, `analytics.md`, and
shared positioning.

## Workflow

### 1. Resolve current company route

Ask for official company identity/domain, sector, public location/team/founding data, permitted people, approved public
funding disclosures, profile owner, legal/finance approver, source URLs, existing Dealroom record, and correction goal.
Browse live route and return `READY TO CLAIM/CORRECT`, `READY TO SUBMIT`, or `BLOCKED`.

### 2. Benchmark five company records

Choose five peers by stage, sector, geography, and business model. Record observed identity, sector, funding/team
display, signal/news/deal context, source cues, and platform-generated fields. Do not infer values from rankings.

### 3. Build field-ready, source-backed facts

After authorized form observation, map every field to a public approved source: company identity, description,
sector/tags, HQ, founded date, approved people, website, public funding/status, and relevant news. Separate public
claims from restricted diligence data; create a correction packet with citations and owner approval.

### 4. Submit/correct and maintain

Show exact fields, sources, disclosure choices, recipients, and submitter for approval. Track profile status,
corrections, accuracy, visible signals, referrals, and material changes. End `LIVE — DEALROOM RECORD GOVERNED`.
