# Launch on AllTopStartups

## Goal

Create `.ulpi/launch/alltopstartups/` with `ROUTE-AND-ELIGIBILITY.md`, `BENCHMARK.md`, `LISTING-PACKET.md`,
`EDITORIAL-PITCH.md`, `COMMERCIAL-DISCLOSURE.md`, `SUBMISSION-CHECKLIST.md`, `FOLLOW-UP-RUNBOOK.md`, `analytics.md`,
and shared positioning.

## Workflow

### 1. Resolve the appropriate route

Collect product URL, official name, owner, category, target reader, launch/news hook, primary sources, approved
claims, visual assets, customer permissions, and budget. Browse the live routes and choose `DIRECTORY READY`,
`EDITORIAL PITCH READY`, `COMMERCIAL OPTION TO REVIEW`, or `NOT A FIT`. The observed directory accepts
business/technology products and services; recheck that criterion and the free-queue status live.

### 2. Benchmark exactly five listings

Use five current, category-relevant profiles. Record category, listing title, description structure, problem,
audience, workflow, access/pricing claim, CTA, date, related-listing context, and review/reply availability. Extract
patterns, not copy. Compare the product only on source-backed evidence.

### 3. Build the listing and pitch packets

Prepare a category recommendation; name; concise outcome-first title/tagline; factual 2–3 paragraph description;
official URL; approved logo/screenshots if the current form requests them; category/tag suggestions; pricing/trial
facts; support/contact route; and correction owner. Make any story pitch news-led: factual hook, reader value,
primary evidence, approved quote, founder/company context, media, and disclosure. Maintain separate commercial terms,
cost, claimed deliverables, dates, cancellation/refund facts, and a no-editorial-guarantee notice.

### 4. Submit only with approval and operate it

Show the exact destination, final packet, form/check-out fields, fees, disclosure, and owner for approval. After a
listing or story is live, verify the rendered name, URL, category, description, images, labels, tracking links, and
claims. Monitor referral sessions, engaged visits, qualified conversions, publication status, review/reply activity,
corrections, and commercial spend independently. End `LIVE — ALLTOPSTARTUPS RUNBOOK ACTIVE`.
