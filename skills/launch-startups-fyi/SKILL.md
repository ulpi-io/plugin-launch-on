# Launch on Startups.fyi

## Goal

Create `.ulpi/launch/startups-fyi/` with `ROUTE-AND-FIT.md`, `BENCHMARK.md`, `FEATURE-PACKET.md`,
`METRIC-DISCLOSURE.md`, `FORM-CAPTURE.md`, `COMMERCIAL-DECISION.md`, `SOURCE-REGISTER.md`, `analytics.md`, and
positioning.

## Workflow

### 1. Assess feature fit

Return `PAID FEATURE READY`, `NEEDS CLEARER PRODUCT EVIDENCE`, or `NOT A FIT`. Confirm the project is live or
honestly described, its product URL resolves, the representative owns the claim set, and any monetary metric is
deliberately public and source-backed.

### 2. Benchmark exactly five peers

Use `$browse` to compare exactly five live listings relevant to product type and stage. Capture observable structure,
description length, CTA, public metric treatment, and product proof. Produce a differentiated factual pitch without
copying language, claiming peer-equivalent outcomes, or manufacturing revenue.

### 3. Build the paid-feature packet

Prepare approved contact owner; company/product name; canonical URL; concise one-line value proposition; customer and
category; product access/pricing; logo/screenshots if requested; founders; public support URL; source register;
optional self-reported metric with period/currency/method/disclosure; and correction owner. Create an explicit
public/private boundary for metrics and email.

### 4. Capture payment and final form details

After approval to proceed, record every current form field and validation, required marker, character/file limit,
price/currency/tax, payment provider, refund/cancellation, review criteria, publication queue, backlink treatment,
and confirmation. Flag discrepancies from the observed $29-starting-price statement for user review.

### 5. Submit with approval and maintain

Present the exact form preview, source mapping, customer-facing copy, public metric disclosure, current commercial
terms, and payment owner for approval. After authorized submission/payment, save receipt/confirmation, verify the
published listing, correct facts with evidence, and measure qualified referral traffic, activation, conversion, and
spend. End `LIVE — STARTUPS.FYI RUNBOOK ACTIVE`.
