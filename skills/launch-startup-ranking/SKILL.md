# Launch on Startup Ranking

## Goal

Create `.ulpi/launch/startup-ranking/` with `ROUTE-AND-ELIGIBILITY.md`, `BENCHMARK.md`,
`PROFILE-EVIDENCE-REGISTER.md`, `CREATE-OR-CORRECT-PACKET.md`, `COMMERCIAL-DECISION.md`, `FORM-CAPTURE.md`,
`CORRECTION-LOG.md`, `analytics.md`, and positioning.

## Workflow

### 1. Choose the correct data action

Return `NEW PROFILE READY`, `CORRECTION READY`, `FASTER APPROVAL OPTION TO REVIEW`, `PREMIUM/BOOSTER/AD OPTION TO
REVIEW`, or `NOT EVIDENCE-READY`. Check for an existing record first and choose correction over duplicate creation.

### 2. Benchmark exactly five peers

Use `$browse` to compare five current comparable startup profiles. Separate observed database values from company
evidence. Produce a factual identity, country, category, and description package; do not optimize content to game SR
Score or rank.

### 3. Build the evidence packet

Prepare canonical name/domain, legal/company identity, launch/founding facts, country/HQ, concise product description,
authorized representative, official social URLs, public contact, independent corroboration, and correction owner.
Label every item first-party, independent, inferred, or unknown. Exclude confidential revenue, customers, or funding.

### 4. Capture fields and commercial offers

After authorized access, record every creation/correction field and validation. For Faster Approval/Premium/Booster/Ads,
capture exact live price, tax, SLA, feature duration, link/placement, disclosure, refund, receipt owner, and outcome
boundaries. Make clear that paid speed/visibility is not a score or rank guarantee.

### 5. Submit with approval and maintain

Present the live route, field-to-source map, duplicate check, final facts, and commercial terms for approval. After
authorized action, retain confirmation, verify the public record, correct drift with authoritative sources, and measure
accuracy, inclusion, qualified referrals, and spend—never rank movement as a promised KPI. End `LIVE — STARTUP RANKING
RUNBOOK ACTIVE`.
