# Launch on SuperLaunch

## Goal

Create `.ulpi/launch/superlaunch/` with `ROUTE-AND-FIT.md`, `BENCHMARK.md`, `LAUNCH-PACKET.md`, `MEDIA-PLAN.md`,
`FOUNDER-PACKET.md`, `REVIEW-RUNBOOK.md`, `FORM-CAPTURE.md`, `analytics.md`, and positioning.

## Workflow

### 1. Confirm AI-product and launch fit

Return `LAUNCH READY`, `NEEDS TESTABLE PRODUCT/ASSETS`, or `NOT A FIT`. Confirm the product works, category is
appropriate, a trial/demo access path is reliable, team consent is documented, and the intended visibility window is
available and honest about launch readiness.

### 2. Benchmark exactly five peer products

Use `$browse` to study five current, comparable products. Compare factual card/page construction, category,
tagline/description, launch-window language, media, rating/review framing, and CTA. Produce a distinct buyer-readable
statement and testable product proof without imitating claims or ratings.

### 3. Assemble the full form package

Prepare required Product Name, Tagline, Product URL, Category, Description, images, Visibility Window, Founder Name,
and Founder Email; plus optional trial URL, video, source, public social links/bio, team members, and newsletter
choice. Validate each URL and asset permission; create alt/caption copy, a source register, FAQ/support destination,
and correction owner.

### 4. Create the honest-review plan

Define a real tester audience, access instructions, supported feedback questions, response SLA, issue triage, and
public correction procedure. Review invitations must solicit candid independent feedback, disclose any relationship,
and avoid incentives tied to sentiment, rating, or timing.

### 5. Submit with approval and operate

Show the exact live form, field-to-source map, selected visibility window, newsletter choice, asset preview, and review
plan for approval. After authorized submission, verify listing/review state, respond transparently, correct facts, and
measure qualified visits, tester activation, feedback themes, product improvements, and conversion. End `LIVE —
SUPERLAUNCH RUNBOOK ACTIVE`.
