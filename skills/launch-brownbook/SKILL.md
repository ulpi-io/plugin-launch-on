# Launch on Brownbook

## Goal

Create `.ulpi/launch/brownbook/` with `DUPLICATE-AND-OWNERSHIP.md`, `BENCHMARK.md`, `ENTITY-PACKET.md`,
`FIELD-MAP.md`, `MEDIA-AND-TAGS.md`, `CLAIM-AND-VERIFICATION.md`, `REVIEW-GOVERNANCE.md`, `analytics.md`, and
positioning.

## Workflow

### 1. Resolve entity and eligibility

Return `NEW LISTING READY`, `CLAIM/CORRECTION FIRST`, `NOT ELIGIBLE`, or `STEP-2 CAPTURE NEEDED`. Establish the legal
or trading business identity, canonical name, control of domain/phone/email, truthful physical or service-area data,
operating country, category, and public contact policy. Search for duplicate profiles and record all results before
deciding a route.

### 2. Benchmark exactly five profiles

Use `$browse` to compare exactly five current, relevant profiles. Capture fields present, claim signal, content
completeness, category/tag choices, public trust evidence, review state, media, and contact presentation. Build a
source-governed field plan that improves clarity without copying peer wording or inflating proof.

### 3. Build the entity and content packet

Prepare approved sources for business name, primary category, address/city/country/postal code, phone, public email,
website/display URL, social profiles, video/contact channels, business and location tags, concise overview, hours if
the current flow asks, logo/photos/video, service scope, and support path. Keep NAP data consistent with the company
site and other authoritative profiles. Use a public support alias rather than personal contact data where appropriate.

### 4. Capture all live fields and claim requirements

After explicit authorization, record the current Step 1, Step 2, sign-in, claim, identity/ownership verification,
media, category, tag, review, and Profile+ controls. Map every entry to a source; re-check mandatory markers, limits,
URLs, consent, pricing, tax, renewal, and cancellation before any paid option. Do not advance the form, submit, or
verify until the user approves the complete preview.

### 5. Publish, verify, and govern the profile

After authorized publication or claim, verify the live canonical URL, claim state, name, category, NAP, links, overview,
tags, media, and any review response channel. Correct mistakes through the owner path. Invite only genuine customers
to give independent feedback, respond factually and privately where necessary, track referral traffic and qualified
contacts without overstating attribution, and maintain a change log. End `LIVE — BROWNBOOK PROFILE RUNBOOK ACTIVE`.
