# Launch on Feedough

## Goal

Create `.ulpi/launch/feedough/` with `ELIGIBILITY.md`, `BENCHMARK.md`, `REVIEW-PACKET.md`,
`TERM-RECONCILIATION.md`, `SOURCE-PACKET.md`, `FORM-CAPTURE.md`, `SUBMISSION-CHECKLIST.md`,
`FOLLOW-UP-RUNBOOK.md`, `analytics.md`, and shared positioning.

## Workflow

### 1. Prove Discovery fit and route readiness

Collect product/company owner, official sources, problem, distinct solution, user, market shift, deployment evidence,
approved media, permissions, and budget. Browse live Feedough routes; compare its current eligibility/disclaimer and
commercial statements. Return `READY TO REQUEST TERMS`, `READY TO SUBMIT`, or `NOT A DISCOVERY FIT`.

### 2. Benchmark exactly five reviews

Record the five peer headlines, editorial angle, target user, pain point, product workflow, sources/claims, review
format, author/date, external links, CTA, and paid/editorial labels. Produce original, source-backed positioning—never
copy a review’s prose or promise comparable treatment.

### 3. Assemble the questionnaire and terms packet

Prepare concise answers covering company/product identity, URL, disruption statement, problem, unique mechanism,
target market, current stage, evidence, approved founder quote, media, and correction contact. Capture every live
questionnaire field only after approval. Maintain a separate written comparison of stated price, deliverables,
distribution, disclosure, timing, cancellation, and editorial-control terms.

### 4. Submit and measure only with approval

Present exact form values, questionnaire destination, evidence, current commercial terms, disclosure, and owner for
approval. After publication, verify title, URL, facts, labels, links, and correction route. Track referred sessions,
engaged visits, qualified conversions, source quality, response/publication status, and any spend separately. End
`LIVE — FEEDOUGH RUNBOOK ACTIVE`.
