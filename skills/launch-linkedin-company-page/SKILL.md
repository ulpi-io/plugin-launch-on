# Launch on LinkedIn Company Pages

## Goal

Create `.ulpi/launch/linkedin-company-page/` with `ROUTE.md`, `BENCHMARK.md`, `PAGE-PACKAGE.md`,
`BRAND-ASSETS.md`, `GOVERNANCE.md`, `UPDATE-PLAN.md`, `CHECKLIST.md`, `analytics.md`, and shared positioning.

## Workflow

### 1. Establish authority and Page route

Ask for legal/display name, domain, owner/admins, exact company facts, logo/banner, industry/type/size, office
locations, founded year, website, specialties, approved description, existing Page URL, employee-association policy,
and support/contact owner. Browse the live setup route and return `READY TO CREATE`, `READY TO CLAIM/UPDATE`, or
`BLOCKED — AUTHORITY/ACCOUNT NEEDED`.

### 2. Benchmark five public Company Pages

Choose five comparable organizations by audience, category, maturity, region, and business model. Record public page
facts, tagline, visual hierarchy, About coverage, CTA, content cadence/themes, employee association, and proof. Label
facts versus inference and do not copy their narrative.

### 3. Prepare exact Page data

After authorized form observation, produce a field-by-field table for name, public URL, website, industry,
organization type, organization size, logo, cover, tagline, description, founded year, locations, specialties, CTA,
and all visible contact/verification options. Prepare accessible asset specs, source-of-truth links, employee guidance,
and approved first updates. Use `$launch-linkedin` separately only for post-launch content.

### 4. Publish and govern

Before a state change, show fields, assets, public visibility, admins, and first-update schedule for approval. Track
Page completeness, branded search/referrals, follower quality, employee associations, engagement, clicks, inquiries,
and admin changes. Review facts quarterly and revoke access promptly on role changes. End `LIVE — COMPANY PAGE
GOVERNED`.
